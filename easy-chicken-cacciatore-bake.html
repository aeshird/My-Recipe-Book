Easy Chicken Cacciatore Bake

Ingredients

  • 1 can (14 1/2 oz) Italian-style diced tomatoes, undrained
  • 2 cups quartered zucchini slices
  • 4 chicken breasts, halves
  • 1/3 cup shake and bake original chicken seasoned coating mix
  • 2 tbsp grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups penne pasta, cooked and drained (about 1 cup dry)

Instructions

  1. Toss tomatoes with zucchini. Coat chicken with 1/3 cup mix as directed on package.
  2. Place chicken over tomatoe mixture in 9x13 pan; sprinkle with cheese.
  3. 375 30-35 min. or until chicken is tender.
  4. Serve over pasta or combine pasta with tomatoes and zucchini.
