Cheesy Tomato Basil Chicken Breasts

Ingredients: Sauce

  • 3 tbsp marg.
  • 2 cups (2 medium) cubed 1 inch tomatoes
  • 1/3 cup chopped onion
  • 6 oz tomato paste
  • 1 tbsp basil leaves
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • 1 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 3 whole chicken breasts, skinned and boneless

Ingredients: Topping

  • 1/2 cup bread crumbs, can
  • 4 tsp parsley flakes
  • 2 tbsp marg., melted
  • 6 oz Mozzarella cheese, cut into strips
  • Stir bread crumbs, parsley flakes, and marg. together.

Instructions

  1. Heat oven to 350.
  2. In 9x13 pan, melt 3 tbsp marg. in oven (4-6 min).
  3. Stir together remaining sauce ingred., except chicken, set aside.
  4. Place chicken in pan, turning to coat with butter.
  5. Spoon sauce mixture over chicken.
  6. Bake 30 to 40 min. or until chicken is no longer pink.
  7. Place cheese strips over chicken; sprinkle with topping mixture.
  8. Continue baking 5 to 10 min. or until chicken is fork tender and bread crumbs are browned.
  9. Serves 6.
