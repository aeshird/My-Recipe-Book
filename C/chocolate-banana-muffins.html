Chocolate Banana Muffins

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups mashed banana
  • 1/3 cup canola oil
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • 1 cup mini chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Line muffin tins with paper liners or spray lightly with cooking spray.
  2. In a medium mixing bowl, combine mashed banana, canola oil, egg, and granulated sugar. Whisk together and set aside.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, combine flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, baking powder, and mini chocolate chips. Blend together with a wire whisk and make a well in the center.
  4. Pour the banana mixture into the well of the dry ingredients. Using a wooden spoon, mix together just until the dry ingredients are moistened. Batter may be lumpy or have floury clumps; this is okay.
  5. Spoon batter into prepared muffin tins, filling each cup about two-thirds full.
  6. Bake on the lower rack of the oven for 20–25 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  7. Remove from oven and place on a wire rack. Let stand for 5 minutes before removing muffins from the pan.

Notes

  • Yield: about 16 muffins.
