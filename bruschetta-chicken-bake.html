Bruschetta Chicken Bake

Ingredients

  • 1 can (14 1/2 oz) diced tomatoes, undrained
  • 1 6 oz pkg stove top suffing mix for chicken
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 gloves garlic, minced
  • 1 1/2 lbs chicken breasts, bite size pieces
  • 1 tsp basil
  • 1 cup Kraft 2% mild shredded mozzarella cheese

Instructions

  1. Mix tomatoes, stuffing mix, water and garlic just until stuffing mix is moistened.
  2. Layer chicken, basil and cheese in 3-qt. casserole or 9x13 baking pan (sprayed).
  3. Top with stuffing - 400 - 30 min. or until chicken is done.
