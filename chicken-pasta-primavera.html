Chicken & Pasta Primavera

Ingredients

  • 1 cr. mushroom soup, Healthy Request
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 3 tbsp grated parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 bag (16 oz) frozen veg. combo: broccoli, cauliflower, carrots
  • 2 cups cubed cooked chicken
  • 4 cups cooked pasta

Instructions

  1. Mix soup, milk, cheese, garlic pwd. and vegetables in saucepan; bring to a boil.
  2. Cover & cook over low heat 10 min. or until crips tender.
  3. Add chicken and heat through.
  4. Serve with pasta.
