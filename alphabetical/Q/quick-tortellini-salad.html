Quick Tortellini Salad

Notes

  • Yield: 8 servings (1/2 cup each)
  • Prep: 25 min
  • Total: 25 min

Dressing

  • 1/3 cup olive oil
  • 3 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp dried oregano leaves

Salad

  • 1 package (9 oz) refrigerated cheese-filled tortellini
  • 1 cup sliced carrots
  • 1 1/2 cups frozen cut green beans (from 1-lb bag)
  • 2 tbsp sliced green onions (2 medium)

Instructions

  1. In a jar with a tight-fitting lid, combine all dressing ingredients. Shake well and set aside.
  2. Cook tortellini with carrots and green beans as directed on tortellini package, until tortellini are tender and vegetables are crisp-tender. Drain and return to saucepan.
  3. Cover with cold water and let stand 5 minutes to cool. Drain well.
  4. In a medium bowl, stir together the tortellini, carrots, and green beans. Add green onions.
  5. Pour dressing over salad and toss gently to coat. Serve chilled or at room temperature.
  6. Note: If doubling noodles, do not double the dressing; 1 1/2 batches (or less) is usually enough.
