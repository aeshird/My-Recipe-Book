Quick Teriyaki Chicken Rice Bowls

Notes

  • Yield: 4 servings
  • Prep: 5 min
  • Cook: 15 min
  • Total: 20 min

Main

  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breast, cut into bite-size pieces
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

For the Sauce

  • 1/4 cup packed light brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup low-sodium soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp rice vinegar or apple cider vinegar
  • 1/2 tsp ground ginger
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch

Additional

  • Cooked rice
  • Steamed broccoli

Instructions

  1. Cook the rice according to the package instructions.
  2. Steam the broccoli until crisp-tender and set aside.
  3. Season the chicken pieces with salt and pepper. Place them in a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat.
  4. Cook the chicken, stirring often, until cooked through and lightly browned.
  5. In a small bowl or measuring cup, combine the brown sugar, soy sauce, vinegar, ginger, garlic, and cornstarch. Whisk until there are no cornstarch lumps.
  6. Add the sauce to the cooked chicken and cook, stirring constantly, until it thickens into a shiny glaze that coats the chicken.
  7. Remove from heat and serve immediately over rice with steamed broccoli.
