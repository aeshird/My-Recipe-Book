Garlic Crushed Red Pepper Chicken Stir Fry

Notes

  • Prep: 5 min
  • Cook: 15 min
  • Total: 20 min

Chicken

  • 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 lbs chicken breasts (about 4), cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp onion powder

Green Beans & Sauce

  • 16 oz fresh green beans, trimmed and halved
  • 1/3 cup coconut aminos
  • 1 tbsp ghee or butter
  • 1 1/2 tsp minced garlic
  • 1/2 tsp ground ginger
  • 1/4 tsp crushed red pepper flakes
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the chicken pieces and season with dried basil, salt, pepper, and onion powder. Sauté, flipping occasionally, for 4–5 minutes, until the chicken has some browning.
  3. Reduce heat to medium. Add coconut aminos, ghee or butter, garlic, ginger, crushed red pepper flakes, and additional salt. Stir to coat the chicken.
  4. Add the halved green beans to the skillet and stir.
  5. Cover and cook on medium to medium-low heat for about 10 minutes, stirring frequently and scraping up the browned bits from the bottom of the pan.
  6. Cook until the chicken is cooked through and the green beans are softened but not totally wilted. Serve hot.
