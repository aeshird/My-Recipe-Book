Grasshopper Cocktail

Notes

  • Yield: 6 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 1/3 cup crème de menthe (plus more for serving, if desired)
  • 1 1/3 cup crème de cacao
  • 40–60 ice cubes
  • 8 pints vanilla ice cream
  • 32 chocolate biscuit sticks

Instructions

  1. In a large blender, combine crème de menthe, crème de cacao, ice cubes, and vanilla ice cream. You may need to work in batches depending on the size of your blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Pour into glasses and, if desired, drizzle a little extra crème de menthe on top.
  4. Serve each glass with chocolate biscuit sticks for dipping or garnish.
