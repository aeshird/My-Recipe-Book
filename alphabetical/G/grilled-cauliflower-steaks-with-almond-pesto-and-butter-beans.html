Grilled Cauliflower Steaks with Almond Pesto & Butter Beans

Notes

  • Yield: 4 servings
  • Prep: 25 min
  • Total: 25 min

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup almonds
  • 5 tbsp chopped flat-leaf parsley, divided (plus more for garnish)
  • 1/4 cup packed fresh basil (plus more for garnish)
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh chives (plus more for garnish)
  • Zest and juice of 1 lemon
  • 5 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil, divided
  • 1 tsp salt, divided
  • 2 large heads cauliflower
  • 2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 medium shallots, minced
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 2 (15 oz) cans no-salt-added butter beans, rinsed
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/4 tsp ground pepper
  • 2 tsp sherry vinegar

Instructions

  1. Preheat grill to medium.
  2. To make the almond pesto, add almonds, 4 tbsp parsley, basil, chives, lemon zest and juice, 2 tbsp olive oil, and 1/4 tsp salt to a food processor. Pulse until chopped and combined, scraping down sides as needed. Set aside.
  3. Place cauliflower heads on a cutting board, stem-side down. Using a large chef’s knife, cut two 1/2-inch-thick slices from the center of each head to make 4 “steaks.” Reserve remaining cauliflower for another use.
  4. Brush the cauliflower steaks with 2 tbsp olive oil. In a small bowl, combine smoked paprika, garlic powder, and 1/2 tsp salt. Sprinkle the mixture on both sides of the steaks.
  5. Grill the cauliflower steaks over medium heat, turning once, until tender and lightly charred, about 5–7 minutes per side.
  6. Meanwhile, heat the remaining 1 tbsp olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add shallots and minced garlic and cook until softened.
  7. Add butter beans, water, remaining 1/4 tsp salt, and pepper. Simmer until heated through and the liquid slightly reduces. Stir in sherry vinegar and remaining 1 tbsp parsley.
  8. Serve grilled cauliflower steaks over the warm butter beans, topped with almond pesto and garnished with additional parsley, basil, and chives.
