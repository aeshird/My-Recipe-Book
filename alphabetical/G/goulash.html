Goulash

Notes

  • Yield: 6 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 box macaroni
  • 2 lbs beef
  • 2 small onions
  • 2 stalks celery
  • 1 pint stewed tomatoes, sauce & paste
  • 1 green pepper
  • 1 red pepper (optional)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook macaroni according to package directions until tender. Drain and set aside.
  2. Chop onions, celery, green pepper, and red pepper (if using).
  3. In a large skillet or pot, fry the beef with onions and celery until the beef is browned and the vegetables are softened. Drain off excess fat if necessary.
  4. Add stewed tomatoes (with sauce and paste), green pepper, and red pepper. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Stir in the cooked macaroni and mix well.
  6. Simmer for about 30 minutes, stirring occasionally, to allow flavors to blend. Serve hot.
