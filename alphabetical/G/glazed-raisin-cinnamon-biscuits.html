Glazed Raisin-Cinnamon Biscuits

Notes

  • Prep: 15 min
  • Total: 25 min

Biscuits

  • 2 1/2 cups Original Bisquick mix
  • 1/2 cup raisins
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon

Vanilla Glaze

  • 2/3 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 tbsp water
  • 1/4 tsp vanilla

Instructions

  1. Heat oven to 450°F.
  2. In a medium bowl, stir together Bisquick mix, raisins, milk, sugar, and cinnamon just until a soft dough forms.
  3. Sprinkle a work surface generously with additional Bisquick mix. Place dough on the surface and gently roll dough in Bisquick to coat. Shape into a ball and knead 10 times.
  4. Roll dough to 1/2-inch thickness. Cut with a 2 1/2-inch round cutter dipped in Bisquick mix.
  5. Place rounds about 2 inches apart on an ungreased cookie sheet.
  6. Bake for 8–10 minutes, or until golden brown.
  7. In a small bowl, mix powdered sugar, water, and vanilla with a spoon until smooth.
  8. Drizzle glaze over warm biscuits. Serve warm.
