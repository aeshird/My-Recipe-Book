Grilled Pineapple Chicken

Notes

  • Yield: 4 servings
  • Prep: 40 min (includes marinating)
  • Cook: 15 min
  • Total: 55 min

Main

  • 1 fresh pineapple, skin removed and cut into rounds, core removed
  • 2 lb boneless chicken thighs or breasts

Marinade

  • 1/2 cup coconut aminos
  • 1/4 cup pineapple juice (or water as a substitute)
  • 1/4 cup tomato paste
  • 4–6 pitted dates, soaked for 10–15 minutes in warm water, then drained
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika

For Serving & Topping

  • White or cauliflower rice
  • Mixed greens
  • Chopped green onions (for topping)

Instructions

  1. Soak the dates in warm water for 10–15 minutes, then drain.
  2. To make the marinade, add coconut aminos, pineapple juice or water, tomato paste, softened dates, garlic powder, chili powder, and smoked paprika to a blender or food processor. Blend until smooth, scraping down the sides as needed.
  3. Place the chicken in a shallow bowl or dish and pour the marinade over it. Toss to coat all pieces evenly. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes or up to 24 hours.
  4. When ready to grill, cut the pineapple into rounds if not already done and remove the tough inner core from each round. Set aside.
  5. Heat your grill to high heat (about 500°F).
  6. Add the marinated chicken and pineapple rounds to the grill. Cook the chicken, flipping once and basting with any remaining marinade, until cooked through and nicely charred on the outside. Grill the pineapple until warmed and grill marks appear.
  7. Serve the grilled chicken and pineapple over white or cauliflower rice with mixed greens. Garnish with chopped green onions.
