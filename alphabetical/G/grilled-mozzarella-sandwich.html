Grilled Mozzarella Sandwich

Ingredients (Sandwich)

  • 4 slices sourdough bread
  • 4 oz mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 1 large tomato, sliced
  • 2 oz roasted red peppers, jarred

Ingredients (Pesto)

  • 1/2 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 2 cloves garlic, peeled
  • 2 tbsp walnuts
  • 2 tbsp grated Parmesan
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1/4 cup olive oil

Instructions

  1. To make the pesto, place basil, garlic, walnuts, and Parmesan in a food processor. Season with salt and pepper.
  2. With the processor running, slowly pour in the olive oil until the mixture is well combined and emulsified. Set aside.
  3. Spread pesto evenly on all 4 slices of bread.
  4. Place two slices of bread, pesto-side up, on a panini maker or in a toaster oven for about 2 minutes to warm.
  5. Top these slices with mozzarella cheese, tomato slices, and roasted red peppers. Leave the panini maker open and allow the cheese to begin melting and the vegetables to soften.
  6. Place the remaining bread slices on top to close the sandwiches, pesto-side down.
  7. Close the panini maker and cook for 2â€“3 minutes, or until the bread is golden and the cheese is melted.
  8. Remove from the panini maker, slice in half, and serve immediately.
  9. Store leftover pesto covered in the refrigerator; it will keep for up to 1 week.
