Turkey Dijon Roll-Ups

Notes

  • Yield: about 18 servings (roll slices)
  • Prep Time: 20 minutes
  • Total Time: 35 minutes (includes chilling)

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp honey mustard
  • 3 herb or plain flour tortillas (8-inch)
  • 1 carrot, shredded
  • 1 package (6 oz) smoked turkey breast slices
  • 3 leaves leaf lettuce
  • 1/4 cup honey Dijon dressing, for serving

Instructions

  1. Spread the honey mustard evenly over each tortilla, all the way to the edges.
  2. Top each tortilla evenly with shredded carrot, smoked turkey slices, and a leaf of lettuce.
  3. Roll each tortilla up tightly. Wrap each roll securely in plastic wrap.
  4. Refrigerate for at least 15 minutes, but no longer than 12 hours.
  5. To serve, unwrap and cut each roll into 3/4-inch slices. Arrange on a platter and serve with honey Dijon dressing for dipping.

Notes / Variations

  • Also great with ham instead of turkey.
  • Try serving with ranch dressing instead of honey Dijon for a different flavor.
