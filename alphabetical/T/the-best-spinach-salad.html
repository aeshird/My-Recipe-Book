The Best Spinach Salad

Notes

  • Yield: 6 servings
  • Prep Time: 15 minutes
  • Cook Time: 1 hour
  • Total Time: 1 hour 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 lb boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • About 1 1/2 cups white wine
  • 4 slices prosciutto
  • 1/2 lb baby spinach
  • 1 cup thinly sliced baby bella mushrooms
  • 1 cup chopped grape tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup crumbled blue cheese
  • 4 hard-boiled eggs, sliced
  • Blue cheese dressing (store-bought or homemade)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 300°F (150°C). Place the chicken breasts on a baking sheet. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with salt and pepper.
  2. Pour white wine into the pan around the chicken so that it is about 1/2 inch high. Bake for 30 minutes, then flip the chicken and continue baking for another 30 minutes, or until cooked through. Remove from the oven and slice or dice the chicken.
  3. After about 40 minutes of the chicken cooking time, place the prosciutto on a parchment-lined baking sheet and place it in the oven alongside the chicken. Bake for about 15 minutes, or until hard and crisp. Let cool, then crumble.
  4. Prepare the blue cheese dressing if making from scratch.
  5. To assemble the salads, divide the baby spinach evenly between plates or storage containers. Top with sliced chicken, chopped grape tomatoes, sliced mushrooms, crumbled blue cheese, crumbled prosciutto, and sliced hard-boiled eggs.
  6. Drizzle with blue cheese dressing just before serving.
