Triple Chocolate Brownie Shake

Notes

  • Yield: 1 serving
  • Approx. 330 Calories

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsweetened vanilla almond milk
  • 1 serving (34 g) CSE Brownie Batter Protein Powder
  • 30 g frozen banana slices
  • 2 tbsp old-fashioned rolled oats
  • 1 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 1/4 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of sea salt
  • 30 extra dark chocolate chips (about 15 g)
  • 6â€“8 ice cubes (about 120 g)

Instructions

  1. Add the almond milk, protein powder, frozen banana slices, rolled oats, cocoa powder, vanilla extract, sea salt, chocolate chips, and ice cubes to a high-powered blender.
  2. Blend on high until completely smooth and thick.
  3. Pour into a glass and serve immediately.
