The Original Acai Bowl

Notes

  • Yield: 1 serving
  • Approx. 335 Calories

Ingredients

  • 1 frozen unsweetened acai pack (such as Sambazon)
  • 50 g frozen strawberries
  • 40 g frozen bananas
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened almond & coconut milk
  • 1 serving CSE Simply Vanilla Protein
  • 4â€“6 ice cubes

Toppings

  • 20 g banana, sliced
  • 1 tbsp sliced almonds
  • 1 tbsp granola

Instructions

  1. Place the frozen acai pack in a blender and pulse until broken up. Scrape down the sides of the blender as needed.
  2. Add the almond & coconut milk, frozen strawberries, frozen bananas, protein powder, and ice to the blender.
  3. Blend until the mixture is smooth and thick, scraping down the sides as needed.
  4. Pour the mixture into a bowl and top with sliced bananas, sliced almonds, and granola.
