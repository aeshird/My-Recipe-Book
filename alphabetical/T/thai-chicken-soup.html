Thai Chicken Soup

Notes

  • Yield: 4 servings
  • Approx. 345 Calories per serving

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp red curry paste
  • 1 1/2 cups light canned coconut milk
  • 1 cup low-sodium chicken stock
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp raw honey
  • 2 tbsp natural peanut butter
  • 10.5 oz cooked, sliced chicken breasts (about 14 oz raw)
  • 1 cup sliced red bell pepper
  • 1/2 cup thinly sliced yellow onion
  • 1 cup peeled, chopped carrots
  • 2 tsp fresh grated ginger
  • 1 1/3 cups cooked white or brown jasmine rice (about 1/2 cup uncooked)

Toppings (per serving)

  • Lime wedge
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a crockpot, whisk together the red curry paste, coconut milk, chicken stock, fish sauce, honey, and peanut butter until smooth and well combined.
  2. Add the cooked sliced chicken, red bell pepper, onions, carrots, and ginger to the crockpot. Stir to combine, cover, and cook on low for 6 hours or on high for 4 hours.
  3. Cook the jasmine rice separately according to the package directions.
  4. Weigh the entire batch of soup and divide by 4 to determine the amount per serving. Ladle each serving into a bowl and top with 1/3 cup cooked rice.
  5. Squeeze a lime wedge over each bowl, garnish with fresh cilantro, and serve warm.
