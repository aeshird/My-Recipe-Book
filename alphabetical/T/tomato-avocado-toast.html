Tomato & Avocado Toast

Ingredients

  • Sourdough bread, sliced and toasted
  • Ripe avocado
  • Feta cheese
  • Cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Lemon juice and lemon zest
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Balsamic vinegar
  • Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Toast the sourdough slices to your liking.
  2. Cut the avocado, remove the pit, and scoop the flesh into a bowl. Mash lightly with a fork, then spread the avocado evenly over the toasted bread.
  3. Layer the cherry tomatoes on top of the avocado. Crumble feta over the tomatoes and add fresh basil leaves.
  4. Drizzle lightly with olive oil and a splash of balsamic vinegar. Add a squeeze of lemon juice and a bit of lemon zest.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste and serve immediately.
