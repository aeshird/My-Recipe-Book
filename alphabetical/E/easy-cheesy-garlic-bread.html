Easy Cheesy Garlic Bread

Notes

  • Yield: 8 servings
  • Prep: 2 min | Cook: 20 min

Ingredients

  • 1 fresh loaf of white bread
  • 6 tbsp butter, softened
  • 1 garlic clove, crushed
  • 1 tsp fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 cup shredded hard mozzarella
  • 1/3 cup grated Parmesan

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a small bowl, combine the softened butter with the crushed garlic and parsley. Stir until well combined.
  3. Cut the loaf of bread in half lengthwise. Spread the garlic-parsley butter evenly over the cut sides of the bread.
  4. Place the bread, cut side up, on a baking sheet. Bake at 350°F for 15 minutes.
  5. Remove the bread from the oven and turn the oven to broil.
  6. Sprinkle the grated Parmesan over the bread, then top with the shredded mozzarella, covering all the way to the edges.
  7. Place the bread under the broiler for 4–5 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbling and has brown patches.
  8. Sprinkle with additional finely chopped parsley, if desired. Slice and serve.
