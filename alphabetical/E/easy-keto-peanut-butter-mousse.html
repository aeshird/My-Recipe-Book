Easy Keto Peanut Butter Mousse

Notes

  • Yield: 6 servings (303 Calories each)
  • Prep: 5 min | Total: 5 min (plus chilling)

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 4 oz cream cheese, softened to room temperature
  • 2 1/4 tbsp natural sugar-free peanut butter
  • 5 tbsp Swerve Confectioners
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla
  • 4 tbsp Lily’s chocolate chips (optional, for topping)

Instructions

  1. Place a medium-sized mixing bowl in the fridge for about 15 minutes to chill. This helps the cream whip faster.
  2. Remove the chilled bowl from the fridge and add the heavy whipping cream. Beat until it almost doubles in size and forms soft peaks.
  3. In a separate mixing bowl, add cream cheese, peanut butter, Swerve, and vanilla. Beat until the mixture is creamy, smooth, and fluffy.
  4. Add the whipped cream to the peanut butter mixture and mix on low speed until thoroughly combined.
  5. Spoon the mousse into serving dishes and refrigerate for about 2 hours to allow it to firm up.
  6. Just before serving, top each portion with Lily’s chocolate chips, if desired.
