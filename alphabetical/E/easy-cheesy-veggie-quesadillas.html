Easy Cheesy Veggie Quesadillas

Notes

  • Yield: 2 servings

Ingredients (Quesadillas)

  • 1 tomato
  • 1/2 onion
  • 1/2 bell pepper
  • 1 cup shredded Mexican blend cheese
  • 2 flour tortillas
  • 1 tbsp room temperature butter

Ingredients (Pico de Gallo)

  • 1 tomato
  • 1/4 onion
  • 1 tbsp freshly squeezed lime juice
  • Few sprigs of cilantro, chopped
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions (Quesadillas)

  1. Chop the tomato, onion, and bell pepper.
  2. Heat a skillet over medium heat. Place a tortilla on your work surface and cover half of it with a layer of cheese. Add a layer of onions, tomatoes, and bell peppers, then top with another layer of cheese. Fold the tortilla in half.
  3. Spread 1/2 tbsp of butter on one side of the folded tortilla. Place it butter-side down in the heated skillet. Spread the remaining 1/2 tbsp butter on the top side.
  4. Cook for 2â€“3 minutes on each side, or until golden brown, crispy, and the cheese is melted.
  5. Remove from the skillet, cut into wedges, and serve with Pico de Gallo (or salsa, guacamole, and/or sour cream).

Instructions (Pico de Gallo)

  1. Chop the onions, tomato, and cilantro.
  2. Add lime juice, salt, and pepper to taste. Mix well and serve alongside quesadillas.
