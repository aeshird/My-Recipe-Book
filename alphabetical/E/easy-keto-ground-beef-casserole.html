Easy Keto Ground Beef Casserole

Notes

  • Yield: 8 servings
  • Prep: 15 min | Cook: 25 min | Total: 40 min

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 3 oz cream cheese
  • 1/2 cup beef broth
  • 1/2 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 2 tsp Bragg’s Aminos
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 cans green beans, drained
  • 3/4 cup cheddar cheese
  • 3/4 cup mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. In a cast-iron skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat, breaking it up as it cooks. Drain excess grease if needed.
  3. Add cream cheese to the skillet and stir until melted and well combined with the beef.
  4. Add beef broth, heavy whipping cream, Bragg’s Aminos, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Stir to combine.
  5. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce heat to medium and cook until it begins to thicken. Reduce heat further and simmer until thickened to your liking.
  6. Once thickened, spread the drained green beans evenly over the top of the beef mixture.
  7. Sprinkle cheddar and mozzarella cheeses evenly over the green beans.
  8. Transfer the skillet to the oven (or pour into a greased baking dish) and bake for 25 minutes, or until hot and bubbly and the cheese is melted.
