Easy Cinnamon Roll Coffee Cake

Notes

  • Yield: 12–14 servings

Ingredients (Cake)

  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 3 cups flour
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 4 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 cups milk

Ingredients (Topping)

  • 1 cup butter, melted
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 1 tbsp cinnamon

Ingredients (Glaze)

  • 2 cups powdered sugar
  • 5 tbsp milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Grease a 9 x 13-inch pan.
  2. In a large bowl, mix all cake ingredients together until well combined. Pour into the prepared pan.
  3. In a small bowl, combine all topping ingredients and mix until well combined. Drop or spread the topping evenly over the cake batter and swirl with a knife.
  4. Bake at 350°F for 30–35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  5. For the glaze, whisk together powdered sugar, milk, and vanilla in a small bowl until it reaches the consistency of pancake syrup.
  6. While the cake is still warm, drizzle the glaze over the top. It will set into a slightly firmer glaze as it cools.
  7. Serve warm or at room temperature.
