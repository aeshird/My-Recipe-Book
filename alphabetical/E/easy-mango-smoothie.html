Easy Mango Smoothie

Notes

  • Yield: 1 large serving
  • Total: 5 min

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp turmeric (optional; leave out for plain mango smoothie)
  • 1 cup frozen mango
  • 3/4 cup yogurt (any kind)
  • 1 frozen banana or 1 cup frozen cauliflower
  • Splash of milk or water (optional, if needed to get the blender going)

Instructions

  1. In a high-speed blender, combine turmeric (if using), frozen mango, yogurt, and frozen banana or cauliflower.
  2. Blend until smooth. If needed, add a splash of milk or water, 1 tablespoon at a time, to help the blender run. Adjust to your preferred thickness.
  3. Pour into a glass and enjoy immediately.
