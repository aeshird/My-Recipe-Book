Egg White, Avocado & Spinach Breakfast Sandwich

Notes

  • Makes 1 breakfast sandwich
  • Prep: 10 min | Cook: 7 min

Ingredients

  • 1 bagel thin (e.g., Thomasâ€™ Everything Bagel Thin or your favorite)
  • 2 egg whites
  • 1 large slice of tomato
  • 4 spinach leaves
  • 1/4 avocado (about 2 tbsp)
  • 1 slice (3/4 oz) Jarlsberg Lite Swiss cheese or your favorite reduced-fat cheese
  • Mustard
  • 1 tsp reduced-fat butter (1/2 tsp for each side of bagel)

Instructions (Panini/Grill)

  1. Preheat a panini maker or George Foreman grill.
  2. Add egg whites to a small microwave-safe bowl or glass custard cup (about 4 3/4 inches). Cover tightly so the egg whites do not splatter. Microwave for about 1 minute 20 seconds, or until set.
  3. Split the bagel thin. Spread 1/2 tsp butter on the outside of each half. Spread mustard on the inside of the top half and mash avocado on the inside of the bottom half.
  4. Place the cooked egg whites on top of the avocado. Layer the tomato slice and spinach leaves on the eggs, then top with the slice of cheese. Place the top half of the bagel on the sandwich.
  5. Cook in the panini maker for about 3 minutes, or until the sandwich is golden brown and the cheese has melted.

Instructions (Stovetop Option)

  1. Coat a nonstick pan with cooking spray and heat over medium-high.
  2. Place the assembled sandwich in the pan and cook for about 3 minutes. Flip, reduce heat to medium, and continue cooking until cheese is melted and both sides are golden brown.
