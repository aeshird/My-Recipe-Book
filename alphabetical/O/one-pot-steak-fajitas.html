One Pot Steak Fajitas

Notes

  • Prep: 10 min
  • Cook: 10 min
  • Total: 20 min

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1.5 lb steak (flank, sirloin, or skirt), thinly sliced into 1/2-inch strips
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp cornstarch
  • Salt, to taste
  • Black pepper, to taste
  • 1/2 cup red bell pepper, sliced into thin strips
  • 1/2 cup green bell pepper, sliced into thin strips
  • 1/2 cup onion, sliced into thin strips
  • 1 to 2 tsp lemon or lime juice

Instructions

  1. Heat the butter and oil in a nonstick pot or pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the steak and sprinkle with the chili powder, paprika, onion powder, garlic powder, cayenne pepper, cumin, cornstarch, salt, and pepper. Mix everything together to coat the steak evenly.
  3. Sauté for about 2 minutes, or until the steak slices are slightly browned and no longer pink and raw on the outside. They will not be fully cooked through yet.
  4. Add the red pepper, green pepper, and onion. Sauté everything together until the steak is cooked through and the vegetables are tender. Finish with lemon or lime juice to taste and serve.
