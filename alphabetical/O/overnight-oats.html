Instructions

  1. For each flavor, add oats, yogurt, milk, vanilla, maple syrup, and the flavor-specific ingredients to a jar or airtight container.
  2. Stir well until everything is thoroughly combined and the oats are fully coated.
  3. Cover and refrigerate overnight, or for at least 4 hours, to allow the oats to soften and the flavors to meld.
  4. In the morning, stir again and adjust consistency with a splash of milk if needed. Add any desired toppings and serve.
