Oven Baked Spaghetti Bolognese

Notes

  • Cook: 50 min

Ingredients

  • 16 oz spaghetti, uncooked
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 jar (16 oz) pasta sauce
  • 12 oz cooked ground beef
  • 1/2 onion, chopped
  • 1/2 cup diced red peppers
  • 1/2 cup diced green peppers
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh Italian parsley
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh basil
  • 3 cups broth
  • 1 tsp oregano or Italian seasoning
  • 1 tsp salt, more or less to taste
  • 1 tsp black pepper, more or less to taste
  • 5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup Parmesan cheese shavings

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large baking dish, add the uncooked spaghetti. Drizzle with olive oil and rub the pasta until it is coated. This helps prevent the pasta from sticking.
  3. Add all the remaining ingredients except for the Parmesan cheese. Stir well to combine.
  4. Cover the baking dish and bake for 45 to 50 minutes, stirring halfway through baking to prevent sticking and ensure even cooking.
  5. Remove from the oven, top with Parmesan cheese shavings, and serve hot.
