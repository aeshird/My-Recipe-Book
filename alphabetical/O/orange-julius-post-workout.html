Orange Julius (Post-Workout)

Notes

  • Yield: 1 serving (250 Calories)

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cashew milk
  • 1/2 cup fresh-squeezed orange juice
  • 1 serving CSE Simply Vanilla or Peaches & Cream Protein Powder
  • 1 serving CSE Super Collagen Mix
  • 30 g frozen banana slices
  • 6 ice cubes

Instructions

  1. Add all of the ingredients to a high-powered blender.
  2. Blend on high until smooth, then pour into a cup and enjoy after a workout.
