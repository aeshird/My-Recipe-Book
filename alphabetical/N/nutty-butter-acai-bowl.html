Nutty Butter Acai Bowl

Notes

  • Yield: 1 serving (335 Calories)

Base

  • 1 frozen unsweetened acai pack (Sambazon)
  • 40 g frozen bananas
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened Almond & Coconut milk
  • 1 serving CSE Simply Vanilla Protein
  • 1/2 tbsp natural peanut butter
  • 4â€“6 ice cubes

Toppings

  • 20 g banana, sliced
  • 20 g strawberries, sliced
  • 1 tbsp granola
  • 1 tsp powdered peanut butter

Instructions

  1. Add the frozen acai pack to a blender and pulse until broken up. Scrape down the sides as needed.
  2. Add almond & coconut milk, frozen bananas, protein powder, peanut butter, and ice. Blend until smooth and thick, like soft-serve.
  3. Pour into a bowl.
  4. Top with sliced banana, sliced strawberries, granola, and powdered peanut butter.
  5. Serve immediately.
