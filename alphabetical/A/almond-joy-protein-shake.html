Almond Joy Protein Shake

Notes

  • 1 serving (345 Calories)

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 serving CSE Brownie Batter Protein Powder
  • 2 tbsp old-fashioned rolled oats
  • 60 g frozen banana slices
  • 1 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 1/2 tbsp OffBeat Midnight Almond Coconut Butter (or natural almond butter)
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp coconut extract
  • 6-8 ice cubes (120 g)

Instructions

  1. Add all of the ingredients to a high-powered blender and blend until smooth.
  2. Pour into a cup and enjoy!
