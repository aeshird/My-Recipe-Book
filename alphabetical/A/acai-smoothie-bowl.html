Acai Smoothie Bowl

Ingredients

  • 1 pack frozen acai (e.g., Sambazon smoothie packs)
  • 1/2 frozen banana
  • Heaping 1/2 to 3/4 cup fresh fruit (e.g., mixed berries, pineapple, or fresh bananas)
  • 1/3 cup coconut milk (any nut milk works too) + more if it's too thick
  • 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tbsp hemp seeds (or any seed you like)
  • 1 tbsp protein powder (optional)

Toppings

  • 1/4 cup granola
  • Handful of berries
  • 1/2 banana, sliced

Instructions

  1. Put all the main ingredients in a blender until smooth. If too thick, just keep adding a little coconut milk until it reaches the desired consistency.
  2. Add your toppings.
