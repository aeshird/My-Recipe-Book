Apple Muffins

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup applesauce
  • 1 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Toppings

  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 2 tbsp sugar

Instructions

  1. Beat egg; stir in milk, oil, and applesauce. Mix in remaining ingredients just until moistened. Batter should/may be lumpy.
  2. Grease 12 muffin cups or line with paper; fill 2/3 full.
  3. Mix cinnamon and sugar. Sprinkle on top.
  4. Bake at 400 degrees for 20-25 minutes or until golden brown.
