Java Banana Shake

Notes

  • Yield: 1 serving (250 Calories)

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsweetened vanilla almond milk
  • 1/2 cup brewed Crio Bru
  • 70 g frozen banana slices
  • 3/4 serving CSE Brownie Batter Protein Powder
  • 1 tsp OffBeat Mocha, Midnight Almond Coconut, Buckeye Brownie Batter, or natural almond butter
  • 6â€“8 ice cubes (about 120 g)

Instructions

  1. Add almond milk, brewed Crio Bru, frozen banana, protein powder, OffBeat flavor or almond butter, and ice cubes to a high-powered blender.
  2. Blend on high until smooth.
  3. Pour into a cup and enjoy.
