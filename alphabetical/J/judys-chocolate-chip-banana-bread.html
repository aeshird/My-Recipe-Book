Judy’s Chocolate Chip Banana Bread

Notes

  • Yield: 1 loaf (16 slices)
  • Prep: 20 min
  • Bake: 1 hr + cooling

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter, softened
  • 1 1/4 cups sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup mashed ripe bananas (about 2 medium)
  • 1/4 cup buttermilk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 3/4 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1/4 cup chopped walnuts (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Grease a 9 x 5-inch loaf pan, line the bottom with parchment paper, and grease the paper.
  2. In a large bowl, beat butter and sugar until crumbly.
  3. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  4. Beat in mashed bananas, buttermilk, and vanilla.
  5. In another bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Stir into the creamed mixture just until combined.
  6. Fold in chocolate chips and walnuts, if using.
  7. Transfer batter to the prepared pan and spread evenly.
  8. Bake at 350°F for 60–65 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  9. Cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then remove to a wire rack and peel off parchment paper. Cool completely before slicing.
  10. Note: To make buttermilk, combine 1/4 cup milk with 3/4 tsp vinegar and let stand for a few minutes.
