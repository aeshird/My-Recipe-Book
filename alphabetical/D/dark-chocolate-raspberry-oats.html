Dark Chocolate Raspberry Oats

Notes

  • Yield: 1 serving (330 Calories)

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup old-fashioned rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup cold water
  • 46 g liquid egg white
  • 10 g OffBeat Buckeye Brownie Batter
  • 3/4 serving CSE Brownie Batter Protein
  • 50 g fresh raspberries
  • 4 g OffBeat Buckeye Brownie Batter (for topping)

Instructions

  1. Add the oats, water, and egg whites to a bowl. Microwave for 90 seconds.
  2. Stir in 10 g of the Buckeye Brownie Butter, then stir in the protein powder until well combined.
  3. Top with fresh raspberries and drizzle the remaining 4 g Buckeye Brownie Butter over the top.
