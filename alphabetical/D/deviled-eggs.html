Deviled Eggs

Notes

  • Yield: 12 servings

Ingredients

  • 6 eggs
  • 1/4 cup Miracle Whip
  • 1 tsp vinegar
  • 1 tsp prepared mustard
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • Dash pepper
  • Paprika, for sprinkling

Instructions

  1. Place eggs in a saucepan and cover with water. Bring to a boil, then simmer until hard-boiled. Cool completely.
  2. Peel the eggs and cut each lengthwise in half. Carefully remove the yolks and place them in a small bowl.
  3. Mash the yolks with a fork. Add Miracle Whip, vinegar, mustard, salt, and pepper; mix until smooth.
  4. Spoon or pipe the yolk mixture back into the egg white halves.
  5. Refrigerate until ready to serve. Sprinkle with paprika just before serving.
