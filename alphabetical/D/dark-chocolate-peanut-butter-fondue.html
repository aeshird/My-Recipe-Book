Dark Chocolate Peanut Butter Fondue

Notes

  • Yield: 8â€“12 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (200 g) dark chocolate chips or Kiss My Keto Dark Chocolate Bar
  • 1/2 cup OffBeat Butters Sweet Classic Peanut Butter

Dipper Ideas

  • 1 lb strawberries
  • 2 bananas, sliced
  • Pretzel thins
  • 1 scotcharoo bar, cut into bite-size pieces

Instructions

  1. Cut the stems off the strawberries, slice the bananas into rounds, and cut the scotcharoo bar into bite-size pieces. Set out the fruit and pretzels with a fondue pot and sticks so everything is ready to use.
  2. Add the chocolate and peanut butter to a saucepan or electric fondue pot over low heat. Stir constantly until completely melted and smooth.
  3. Transfer to the fondue pot (if using a saucepan) and start dipping!
