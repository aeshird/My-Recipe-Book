Spinach Lasagna Roll-Ups

Notes

  • Prep: 20 min | Cook: 25 min | Total: 45 min
  • Yield: 8 servings

Tomato Sauce

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 1 (28 oz) can crushed tomatoes
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 3 teaspoons dried basil
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 2 teaspoons dried parsley
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Filling

  • 1 (10 oz) package frozen spinach, thawed
  • 15 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 2 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
  • 2/3 cup shredded Parmesan cheese
  • 1 large egg

Noodles

  • 12 lasagna noodles

Instructions

  1. Prepare the tomato sauce: Heat the olive oil in a medium saucepan over medium heat. Add the diced onion and cook, stirring, until the onions are crisp and translucent.
  2. Add the crushed tomatoes, minced garlic, dried basil, oregano, and parsley. Season with salt and pepper. Simmer for about 20 minutes, stirring occasionally. Remove from the heat and set aside.
  3. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and lay them out on a lightly oiled baking sheet or parchment to prevent sticking while you prepare the filling.
  4. Squeeze the excess moisture from the thawed spinach. In a large bowl, combine the spinach, ricotta, 2 cups of the mozzarella, the Parmesan, and the egg. Mix until well combined. Season with a little salt and pepper if desired.
  5. Preheat the oven to 375°F. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce over the bottom of a 9 x 13-inch baking dish.
  6. Spread a generous spoonful of the cheese and spinach filling over each cooked lasagna noodle. Roll each noodle up from the short end to form a spiral.
  7. Place the roll-ups seam-side down in the prepared baking dish. Pour the remaining tomato sauce evenly over the top of the roll-ups and sprinkle with the remaining 1/2 cup mozzarella.
  8. Cover the dish with foil and bake for 20 minutes. Remove the foil and bake an additional 5–10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and lightly browned and the sauce is bubbling.
  9. Let the roll-ups rest for a few minutes before serving. Serve warm.
