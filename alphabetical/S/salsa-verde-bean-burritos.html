Salsa Verde Bean Burritos

Notes

  • Yield: 4 servings (350 Calories each)

Ingredients

  • 4 oz yellow bell pepper
  • 2 oz yellow onions
  • 2 cups white cannellini beans, canned
  • 4 oz Miyoko's Creamery Fresh Vegan Mozzarella
  • 1 cup salsa verde (green salsa)
  • 4 pieces Joseph’s Flax, Oat Bran, & Whole Wheat Lavash Bread

Toppings (Per Serving)

  • 15 g avocado
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • Fresh cilantro, to garnish

Instructions

  1. Chop the bell pepper and onions. Grease a small frying pan with cooking spray and place over medium heat. Add the bell peppers and onion and sauté until tender and fragrant. Remove from heat.
  2. Drain and rinse the beans, then add them to a mixing bowl with the vegan mozzarella, salsa verde, bell pepper, and onions. Mash the beans and cheese slightly and mix until well combined.
  3. Weigh the mixture and divide by four to find the amount needed for each burrito.
  4. Place one portion of the bean mixture into each Lavash. Roll up tightly.
  5. Place the burritos on a greased skillet or frying pan over medium heat. Cook on each side until browned and crispy.
  6. Top each burrito with 1 cup lettuce, 15 g avocado, and fresh cilantro. Serve warm.
