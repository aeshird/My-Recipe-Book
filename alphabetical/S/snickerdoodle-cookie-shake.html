Snickerdoodle Cookie Shake

Notes

  • Yield: 1 serving (340 Calories)

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsweetened cashew milk
  • 1 serving CSE Snickerdoodle Protein Powder
  • 40 g frozen banana slices
  • 2 tablespoons old-fashioned rolled oats
  • 18 g OffBeat Cinnamon Bun Butter or natural almond butter
  • 6â€“8 ice cubes (about 120 g)

Topping

  • Pinch of turbinado sugar
  • Dash of cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Add the cashew milk, protein powder, frozen banana slices, oats, and nut butter to a high-powered blender.
  2. Blend on high until completely smooth and creamy.
  3. Pour into a glass.
  4. Top with a pinch of turbinado sugar and a dash of cinnamon. Enjoy!
