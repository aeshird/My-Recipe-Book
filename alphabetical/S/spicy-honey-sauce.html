Spicy Honey Sauce

Notes

  • Yield: 16 servings

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup raw honey
  • 1 tablespoon Frankâ€™s buffalo hot sauce
  • Pinch of crushed red pepper flakes, to taste

Instructions

  1. Place the honey, hot sauce, and crushed red pepper flakes in a bowl.
  2. Stir until everything is well combined.
  3. Use immediately or store in an airtight container. This sauce is great on pizzas, cornbread, homemade chicken nuggets, and grilled meats.
