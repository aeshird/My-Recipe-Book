Swedish Meatballs

Notes

  • Servings: 4
  • Uses reserved meatballs from Mini Meatloaves

Ingredients

  • Reserved beef gravy from Mini Meatloaves (about 1 1/4 cups)
  • 1/2 cup sour cream (Breakstone’s or Knudsen)
  • 16 meatballs, reserved from Mini Meatloaves recipe
  • Cooked egg noodles, mashed potatoes, or rice, for serving (optional)

Instructions

  1. Pour the reserved beef gravy into a medium saucepan and heat over medium heat until hot and just beginning to simmer.
  2. Reduce heat to low and whisk in the sour cream until smooth. Do not allow the sauce to boil after adding sour cream.
  3. Add the reserved meatballs to the pan and gently stir to coat them in the sauce.
  4. Simmer on low for 10–15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the meatballs are heated through.
  5. Serve the Swedish meatballs over egg noodles, potatoes, or rice if desired.
