Spinach Cheese Calzones

Notes

  • Total Time: 40 min
  • Yield: 6 servings

Filling

  • 1 (9 oz) package frozen spinach, thawed and well drained
  • 1 cup light ricotta cheese
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil leaves
  • 1 egg white

Crust

  • 1 (10 oz) can refrigerated pizza crust

Sauce

  • 1 (8 oz) can tomato sauce
  • 1 tablespoon grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano leaves

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F. Spray a cookie sheet with nonstick cooking spray.
  2. In a medium bowl, combine all of the filling ingredients (spinach, ricotta, garlic, basil, and egg white) and mix well.
  3. Unroll the pizza dough. Pat or roll the dough into a 12 x 8-inch rectangle. Cut into six 4-inch squares.
  4. Spoon about 1/4 cup of the filling onto the center of each square. Fold the dough diagonally over the filling to form triangles and seal the edges with a fork.
  5. Place the calzones on the prepared cookie sheet. Bake at 400°F for 10–15 minutes, or until the crust is deep golden brown.
  6. Meanwhile, in a small saucepan, combine all of the sauce ingredients. Cook over medium heat until thoroughly heated.
  7. Serve the hot calzones with warm tomato sauce spooned over the top or on the side for dipping.
