Spinach & Feta Turkey Meatballs with Herbed Quinoa

Notes

  • Prep: 35 min | Total: 35 min
  • Yield: 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 cup frozen chopped spinach, thawed
  • 1 pound 93%-lean ground turkey
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 3/8 teaspoon salt, divided
  • 3/8 teaspoon ground pepper, divided
  • 2 cups cooked quinoa, cooled
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 cup chopped parsley
  • 3 tablespoons chopped mint
  • 2 cups sliced cucumber
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup tzatziki

Instructions

  1. Squeeze the excess moisture from the thawed spinach. In a medium bowl, combine the spinach, ground turkey, feta, garlic powder, oregano, 1/8 teaspoon of the salt, and 1/8 teaspoon of the pepper. Mix well.
  2. Form the mixture into 12 meatballs. Heat a large nonstick skillet over medium heat and coat with cooking spray. Working in batches if necessary, add the meatballs and cook until browned on all sides and no longer pink in the center, about 10–12 minutes (an instant-read thermometer should register 165°F). Set the meatballs aside to cool slightly.
  3. In a medium bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, lemon juice, olive oil, parsley, mint, and the remaining 1/4 teaspoon each salt and pepper. Divide the quinoa among 4 single-serving containers.
  4. Top each container with 3 meatballs, 1/2 cup sliced cucumbers, and 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes.
  5. Divide the tzatziki among 4 small containers. Seal everything and refrigerate for up to 4 days.
  6. Before serving, transfer the meatballs to a microwave-safe dish and reheat if desired. Serve with the quinoa, vegetables, and tzatziki.
