Strawberry Feta Spinach Salad

Ingredients

  • 4 cups spinach
  • 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 1/8 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 cups strawberries, sliced
  • 1 cup walnuts
  • 1/2 cup feta cheese

Instructions

  1. Place the spinach, olive oil, balsamic vinegar, and black pepper into a large salad bowl.
  2. Use tongs to toss until the spinach is evenly coated.
  3. Add the sliced strawberries and walnuts to the bowl.
  4. Toss again to combine.
  5. Add the feta and toss gently.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy!
