S’mores Cookie Pie

Notes

  • Yield: 12 servings (200 Calories each)
  • Oven/Grill: 350°F (Traeger or oven)

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup melted coconut oil
  • 1/4 cup coconut sugar
  • 2 tbsp raw honey
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup all-purpose unbleached wheat flour
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • Pinch of sea salt
  • 1 package (16) Cinnamon Churro SmashMallows
  • 1 package (10) Unreal Dark Chocolate Peanut Butter Cups
  • 3 tbsp water

Instructions

  1. Heat the Traeger grill (or oven) to 350°F (176°C).
  2. In a bowl, combine melted coconut oil, coconut sugar, raw honey, vanilla, and water. Beat until well combined.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together flour, cinnamon, baking soda, and sea salt. Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and mix until a soft dough forms. If the mixture seems too dry, add a small splash of water.
  4. Spray a pie pan or cast-iron skillet with cooking spray. Press the dough into the center and up the sides to create a cookie crust.
  5. Place on the Traeger (or in the oven) and bake for 8 to 10 minutes, until just set.
  6. Cut the peanut butter cups in half and press them into the warm cookie crust while still on the grill. Top with SmashMallows.
  7. Bake for another 5 minutes, or until the marshmallows are puffed and lightly browned. Remove from the grill and serve hot.
