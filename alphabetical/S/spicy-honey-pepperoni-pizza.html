Spicy Honey Pepperoni Pizza

Notes

  • Yield: 2 servings (530 Calories per serving)

Ingredients

  • 8 oz Honey Wheat Pizza Crust Dough (see “Honey Wheat Pizza Crust Dough”)

Pizza Toppings

  • 1/2 cup traditional pizza sauce
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella & provolone cheese blend
  • 34 slices (60 g) turkey pepperoni
  • 1/4 cup shredded Parmesan cheese

Sauce

  • 1 tablespoon Spicy Honey Sauce (see “Spicy Honey Sauce”)

Instructions

  1. Heat the Traeger (or grill/oven) to 350°F. Prepare the Honey Wheat Pizza Dough according to its recipe.
  2. While the dough is rising, make the Spicy Honey Sauce and set aside.
  3. Weigh out 8 oz of the pizza dough. On a lightly floured surface, roll it into a circle about 8 inches across. Fold in the edges to create a crust.
  4. Place the dough directly onto the preheated Traeger. Spread the pizza sauce over the dough, then top with the mozzarella & provolone, pepperoni, and Parmesan.
  5. Cook for 10–15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly, rotating the pizza as needed for even cooking.
  6. Remove the pizza from the grill. Drizzle with Spicy Honey Sauce, slice, and enjoy.
