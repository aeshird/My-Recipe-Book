Breakfast Sandwich

Ingredients

  • Bread, toasted
  • Egg
  • Tomato slices
  • Avocado
  • Spinach
  • Feta
  • Cream cheese

Instructions

  1. Toast the bread and cook the egg to your liking.
  2. Spread cream cheese on both pieces of toast.
  3. Layer tomato, avocado, spinach, and feta on the bottom slice.
  4. Top with the cooked egg, then place the remaining piece of toast on top.
  5. Cut the sandwich in half and serve.
