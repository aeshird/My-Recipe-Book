Brownie Batter Buddies

Notes

  • Yield: 16 servings (185 calories per serving)

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup raw honey
  • 1/2 cup natural peanut butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 serving CSE Brownie Batter Protein Powder
  • 1 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 8 cups Rice Chex cereal
  • 1 cup powdered peanut butter

Instructions

  1. In a small saucepan over low heat, melt together the honey, peanut butter, and vanilla. Stir until smooth, then remove from heat.
  2. Stir in the protein powder and cocoa powder until fully combined.
  3. Place the Chex cereal in a large bowl. Pour the hot mixture over the cereal and gently stir until all pieces are coated.
  4. Transfer the coated cereal to a large zip-top bag. Add the powdered peanut butter, seal the bag, and shake until the cereal is well coated.
  5. Spread the mixture out onto wax or parchment paper to cool. Store leftovers in the refrigerator.
