Breakfast Tacos

Notes

  • Yield: 2 servings (350 calories per serving)
  • Each serving: 2 tacos + 1 orange

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • 6 egg whites
  • 1 cup chopped spinach
  • 2 light Laughing Cow cheese wedges
  • 4 corn tortillas
  • 2 tbsp salsa
  • 2 clementine oranges

Instructions

  1. Scramble the eggs and egg whites together with the spinach and cheese wedges in a pan until fully cooked.
  2. Divide the egg mixture evenly among the four tortillas.
  3. Top each taco with 1/2 tbsp salsa.
  4. Serve 2 tacos per person with 1 clementine orange on the side.
