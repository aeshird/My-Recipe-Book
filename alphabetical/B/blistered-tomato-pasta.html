Blistered Tomato Pasta

Notes

  • Yield: 4 servings (532 calories per serving)
  • Prep: 10 min | Cook: 10 min | Total: 20 min

Ingredients

  • 16 oz grape tomatoes
  • 3 tbsp olive oil, divided
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 7 oz fresh arugula
  • 16 oz gluten-free pasta (or pasta of choice)
  • 1/4 tsp kosher salt
  • 1/4 tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tbsp freshly grated Parmesan
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Reserve 2 tbsp of pasta water, then drain and set aside.
  2. In a cast iron skillet over medium-high heat, add 1 tbsp olive oil. Add the grape tomatoes and let them cook undisturbed for 2 minutes. Stir and cook an additional 3 minutes, until the skins are charred/blistered.
  3. Lower the heat to medium. Add the minced garlic, arugula, remaining 2 tbsp olive oil, salt, and pepper. Saut√© for about 3 minutes, until the arugula begins to wilt.
  4. Add the cooked pasta, reserved pasta water, Parmesan, and red pepper flakes (if using). Toss everything together, taste, adjust seasoning, and serve.

Notes

  • You can use cherry tomatoes instead of grape tomatoes, but they have higher water content and thinner skin.
  • The blistered tomatoes alone make a great topping or side.
  • This dish can also be served cold as a pasta salad.
