Baked Ravioli Casserole

Ingredients

  • 1 lb lean ground beef
  • 1 jar spaghetti sauce (approximately 24 oz)
  • 1 bag square-shaped cheese ravioli (25 oz, frozen – no need to thaw)
  • 1 bag shredded mozzarella cheese (8 oz), divided in thirds

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F.
  2. In a medium skillet, crumble and brown the ground beef over medium heat until completely browned. Drain off any excess fat.
  3. Reserve 3/4 cup of the spaghetti sauce and set aside. Add the remaining sauce to the skillet with the ground beef. Stir well, then cover and simmer over medium-low heat for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Spread 1/4 cup of the reserved spaghetti sauce into the bottom of a 9x13-inch glass baking dish. Arrange one third of the frozen ravioli in a single layer in the pan.
  5. Top the ravioli with half of the meat sauce mixture, then sprinkle with one third of the shredded mozzarella cheese.
  6. Place another third of the frozen ravioli in a single layer over the meat sauce and cheese. Top with the remaining meat sauce and another third of the shredded mozzarella.
  7. Place the final third of the frozen ravioli on top. Spoon the remaining reserved spaghetti sauce over the top and sprinkle with the remaining mozzarella.
  8. Bake at 350°F for 30–40 minutes, or until the cheese is completely melted and the casserole is bubbling around the edges.
