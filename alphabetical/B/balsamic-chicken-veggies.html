Balsamic Chicken & Veggies

Notes

  • Yield: 4 servings (360 calories per serving)

Ingredients

Main

  • 1/2 cup cooked quinoa (about 4 tbsp uncooked)
  • 14 oz raw chicken breast (about 10.5 oz cooked)
  • 12 oz green beans, halved
  • 6 oz cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 6 oz matchstick carrots

Balsamic Marinade

  • 3 tbsp avocado or olive oil
  • 4 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tbsp raw honey
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • Sea salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook quinoa according to the directions on the package.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the oil, balsamic vinegar, honey, Dijon mustard, sea salt, and pepper until well combined. Set aside.
  3. Heat a frying pan over medium-high heat and spray with cooking spray. Lightly salt and pepper the chicken, then add it to the pan. Cook 5–7 minutes per side, or until cooked through. Transfer to a plate.
  4. Halve the green beans and tomatoes. Spray the pan again with cooking spray, then add the carrots and green beans. Drizzle half of the balsamic marinade over the vegetables and season with salt and pepper. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, for 5–7 minutes, or until crisp-tender (steam green beans first if you prefer them softer).
  5. Turn the heat down to low. Add the cooked chicken, quinoa, and tomatoes to the pan. Drizzle with the remaining marinade and cook until heated through. Divide into four servings and enjoy warm.
