Buckeye Brownie Bowl

Notes

  • Yield: 1 serving (367 calories)

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup old-fashioned rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 46 g liquid egg whites
  • 14 g OffBeat Buckeye Brownie Peanut Butter
  • 3/4 serving (24 g) CSE Brownie Batter Protein Powder
  • 1 tbsp (6 g) powdered peanut butter
  • 5 g chocolate chips
  • 20 g banana

Instructions

  1. Add oats, water, and egg whites to a bowl. Microwave for 1Â½ minutes.
  2. Stir in 14 g OffBeat Buckeye Brownie Butter until melted.
  3. Add 3/4 serving of CSE Brownie Batter Protein Powder and mix until well combined.
  4. Top with powdered peanut butter, banana slices, and chocolate chips. Serve warm.
