Barbeque Chicken Casserole

Ingredients

  • 1 pkg. Simply Potatoes Shredded Hash Browns
  • 1 med. onion, chopped
  • 1 cup shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 2 tsp. olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1/4 tsp. pepper
  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 1-1/4 cups barbeque sauce

Instructions

  1. Place potatoes in a greased 9 x 13 baking dish.
  2. Stir in onion, cheese, oil, salt and pepper.
  3. Arrange chicken top of potatoes; spoon barbeque sauce evenly over chicken.
  4. Cover with foil.
  5. 400 - approx. 45 min.
