Beef & Broccoli

Ingredients

Main

  • 1 lb flank steak, sliced into 1/4-inch thick strips
  • 3 cups small broccoli florets
  • 1/2 cup beef broth
  • 5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil

Sauce

  • 1/2 cup low-sodium soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tsp cornstarch

Instructions

  1. Toss the sliced beef in a large bowl with 2 tbsp cornstarch until well coated; set aside.
  2. Heat the oil in a pan over medium heat until hot. Add the beef and cook until browned, stirring frequently. Transfer the beef to a plate.
  3. Add the broccoli florets and minced garlic to the now-empty pan and stir. Add the beef broth. Simmer, stirring occasionally, until the broccoli is tender, about 10 minutes.
  4. While the broccoli cooks, stir together the soy sauce, brown sugar, and 2 tsp cornstarch in a bowl until well mixed.
  5. Once the broccoli is tender, return the beef to the pan and pour the sauce over the top. Stir until everything is coated and simmer for about 5 minutes, until the sauce thickens slightly. Serve.
