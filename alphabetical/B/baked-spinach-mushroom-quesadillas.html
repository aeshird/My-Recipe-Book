Baked Spinach Mushroom Quesadillas

Notes

  • Prep: 5 min | Cook: 15 min | Total: 20 min
  • Yield: 4 servings (305 calories per serving)

Ingredients

  • 4 tortillas (8-inch)
  • Olive oil (optional, enough to lightly coat tortillas)
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 3 cups roughly chopped spinach
  • 8 oz sliced mushrooms

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F.
  2. Place the sliced mushrooms in a microwave-safe bowl and microwave for 2½–3 minutes to soften. Drain and set aside.
  3. Lightly brush one side of each tortilla with olive oil (optional) and place them on a baking sheet, oiled side down.
  4. Distribute about half of each cheese over half of each tortilla. Layer the spinach, then mushrooms, and finish with the remaining cheeses. Fold the empty half of each tortilla over the filling.
  5. Bake for about 6 minutes. Flip each quesadilla and bake an additional 6–7 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and the tortillas are golden brown.
