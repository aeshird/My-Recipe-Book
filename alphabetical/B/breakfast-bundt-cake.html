Breakfast Bundt Cake

Notes

  • Yield: 10 servings
  • Prep: 10 min | Cook: ~40 min | Total: ~47 min

Ingredients

  • 2 cans refrigerated biscuits (or 2 cans regular for less bready)
  • 5 eggs
  • 1 cup bacon bits or crushed pre-cooked bacon
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese (sharp recommended)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1/2 onion, diced (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F. Cut each biscuit into 6 pieces and place in a large bowl.
  2. Whisk the eggs with salt, pepper, and diced onion or scallions (if using). Pour over biscuits.
  3. Cook the bacon if not already cooked, crumble, and add to the bowl. Add the shredded cheese.
  4. Use a spoon or your hands to gently mix everything together, keeping biscuit pieces as intact as possible.
  5. Spray the inside of a Bundt pan very well with non-stick spray. Pour the mixture into the pan and spread evenly.
  6. Bake for about 40 minutes, or until the top is a beautiful golden brown.
  7. Let cool slightly before serving. Loosen sides well before flipping over so it slides out cleanly.
