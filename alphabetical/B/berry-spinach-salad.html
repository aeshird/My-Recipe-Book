Berry Spinach Salad

Ingredients

Dressing

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp dry mustard
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp celery seed (optional)
  • 3 tbsp grated onion (or 2 scant tbsp minced onion)
  • 1/3 cup red wine vinegar
  • 1 cup vegetable oil

Salad

  • 2 bunches (about 20 oz) spinach leaves, torn into bite-size pieces
  • 1 pint fresh strawberries, hulled and halved

Instructions

  1. In a large salad bowl, combine the spinach and strawberries. Refrigerate until ready to serve.
  2. In a small bowl, combine the dressing ingredients except for the vegetable oil. Slowly drizzle in the oil while whisking constantly until the dressing is slightly thickened. (You can also blend the dressing in a food processor or blender.)
  3. Just before serving, pour the dressing over the spinach and strawberries, toss well, and serve.

Notes

  • A whisk works well to blend the dressing.
  • Mixing in a 4-cup glass measuring cup with a spout makes pouring easier.
