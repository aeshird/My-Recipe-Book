Loaded Chicken Quesadillas

Notes

  • Yield: 4 servings (350 Calories per serving)

Quesadillas

  • 6 oz cooked chicken breast (about 8 oz raw), shredded
  • 1/2 cup chopped red bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup chopped yellow onions
  • 1/4 cup chopped jalapeño (optional, for heat)
  • 3/4 cup black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1/4 cup frozen sweet corn
  • 1 cup chopped baby kale
  • 1 tbsp taco seasoning
  • 2 whole-grain tortillas (70 g each) per serving, total 8 tortillas
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 100 g avocado

Cilantro-Lime Crema

  • 1/4 cup nonfat plain Greek yogurt
  • 2 tbsp green enchilada sauce
  • 2 tbsp olive oil mayo
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • Juice and zest of 1/2 lime

Instructions

  1. Cook and shred the chicken according to your preferred method.
  2. Prepare the Cilantro-Lime Crema: add all crema ingredients to a blender or food processor and blend until smooth. Weigh the mixture and divide by four to determine the amount needed per serving. Refrigerate until ready to use.
  3. Heat a greased frying pan over medium heat. Add chopped bell peppers, onions, and jalapeño. Sauté until the vegetables are tender.
  4. Add the black beans, corn, kale, shredded chicken, and taco seasoning to the pan. Stir together and cook until the kale is wilted and everything is heated through. Remove from heat.
  5. Heat a clean skillet over medium heat. Place a tortilla in the skillet and spread some of the chicken-veggie mixture over half of it. Sprinkle with shredded mozzarella.
  6. Fold the tortilla over to enclose the filling. Cook for 2–3 minutes per side, until the tortilla is golden brown and the cheese is melted.
  7. Repeat with remaining tortillas and filling.
  8. Slice the avocado and portion evenly over the quesadillas when serving.
  9. Serve each portion of quesadilla with a dollop of Cilantro-Lime Crema on top or on the side.
