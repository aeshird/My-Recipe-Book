Low Carb Eggplant Pizza

Notes

  • Yield: 2 servings (331 Calories each)
  • Prep: 10 min | Cook: 15 min | Total: 25 min

Ingredients

  • 1 large eggplant
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2–1 cup no-sugar-added pizza sauce
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/2 yellow onion, sliced
  • 1 cup fresh baby spinach
  • Sea salt, to taste
  • Ground pepper, to taste
  • 3/4 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh oregano
  • Crushed red pepper, optional

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F.
  2. Slice the eggplant lengthwise into 1/4–1/3-inch thick slices. Brush or rub both sides of each slice with olive oil and place on a parchment-lined baking sheet. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
  3. Bake for 7–10 minutes, or until the eggplant is hot and starting to cook down and soften. Remove from the oven.
  4. While the eggplant bakes, heat the remaining 1/2 tbsp olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add the garlic and onion and sauté for 3–4 minutes until softened. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Add the pizza sauce and spinach to the skillet and cook for 1–2 minutes, until the sauce is warm and the spinach has wilted. Remove from heat.
  6. Spoon the onion, spinach, and sauce mixture over each eggplant slice.
  7. Top each slice with shredded mozzarella and chopped fresh oregano.
  8. Return to the oven and bake for 5–8 more minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  9. Sprinkle with crushed red pepper if desired, and serve immediately.
