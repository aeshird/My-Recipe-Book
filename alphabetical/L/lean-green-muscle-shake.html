Lean Green Muscle Shake

Notes

  • Yield: 1 serving (235 Calories)

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 60 g frozen banana slices
  • 1/2 tbsp natural peanut butter
  • 1/2 serving (16 g) CSE Protein Powder (any flavor)
  • 1 serving (9 g) CSE Super Greens Mix
  • 6â€“8 ice cubes (about 120 g)

Instructions

  1. Add all ingredients to a high-powered blender.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Pour into a glass and enjoy.
