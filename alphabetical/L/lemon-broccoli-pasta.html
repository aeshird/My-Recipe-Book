Lemon Broccoli Pasta

Notes

  • Yield: 6 servings
  • Prep: 5 min | Cook: 10 min | Total: 15 min

Ingredients

  • 3 quarts water
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1 1/2 lbs fresh broccoli
  • 1 lb rotini pasta
  • 3–4 cups spinach
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 1 large lemon, zested and juiced
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed and minced
  • 1/4 tsp crushed red pepper
  • 1 cup freshly grated Parmesan, plus more for garnish
  • Olive oil, for drizzling
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Lemon wedges, for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, bring the 3 quarts of water and 1 tbsp salt to a boil.
  2. While the water heats, trim the broccoli stems and cut the florets into bite-size pieces.
  3. Add the rotini to the boiling water and cook on high for 4 minutes. Add the broccoli, cover, and continue boiling for 3 more minutes.
  4. Drain the pasta and broccoli using a colander or lid. Return them to the pot and stir in the spinach and lemon zest. Let sit a few minutes to allow the spinach to wilt.
  5. Meanwhile, in a small skillet, melt the 4 tbsp butter over medium heat (you can brown it slightly if desired). Add the minced garlic and crushed red pepper and sauté for about 1 minute, until fragrant. Turn off heat and stir in 2–3 tbsp fresh lemon juice.
  6. Pour the lemon-garlic butter over the pasta mixture and toss to coat.
  7. Stir in 1 cup Parmesan and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Drizzle with a little olive oil, garnish with extra Parmesan and lemon wedges, and serve warm.
