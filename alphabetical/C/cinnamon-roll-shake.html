Cinnamon Roll Shake

Notes

  • Yield: 1 serving (350 Calories)

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/4 cup nonfat, plain Greek yogurt
  • 3/4 serving CSE Simply Vanilla Protein Powder
  • 50 g frozen banana slices
  • 2 tbsp old-fashioned rolled oats
  • 1 tbsp OffBeat Cinnamon Bun Butter or 3 tbsp Neufchâtel cream cheese
  • 1 tsp coconut sugar
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 6–8 ice cubes (120 g)

Instructions

  1. Place all the ingredients into a high-powered blender.
  2. Blend on high until smooth.
  3. Pour into a cup and enjoy.
