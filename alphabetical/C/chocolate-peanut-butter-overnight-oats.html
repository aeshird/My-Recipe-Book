Chocolate Peanut Butter Overnight Oats (Vegan)

Notes

  • Yield: 4 servings (406 Calories)
  • Prep: 5 min | Total: 2 hr 5 min

Ingredients

  • 2 cups gluten-free, old-fashioned oats
  • 1 1/2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 2 cups almond, soy, or cashew milk
  • 1/3 cup maple syrup
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1/4 cup peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup roasted peanuts, coarsely chopped

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together oats and cocoa powder.
  2. Add milk, maple syrup, and vanilla. Stir until well combined.
  3. Divide the oat mixture evenly into four 8â€“16 oz mason jars.
  4. Top each jar with 1 tablespoon of peanut butter.
  5. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours before serving.
  6. Just before serving, add 1 tablespoon chopped peanuts to each jar and enjoy!
