Creamy Tuna-Noodle Casserole

Notes

  • Yield: 6 servings
  • Prep: 20 min | Bake: 25 min

Ingredients

  • 5 cups uncooked egg noodles
  • 1 cup frozen peas (or 1 can peas)
  • 1 can (10 3/4 oz) reduced-fat, reduced-sodium condensed cream of mushroom soup, undiluted
  • 1 cup (8 oz) fat-free sour cream
  • 2/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/3 cup 2% milk
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 2 cans (5 oz each) light tuna in water, drained and flaked
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped green pepper

Topping

  • 1/2 cup soft breadcrumbs
  • 1 tbsp butter, melted

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Cook egg noodles according to package directions for al dente, adding frozen peas during the last minute of cooking. Drain well.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the soup, sour cream, Parmesan cheese, milk, and salt. Stir in tuna, onion, and green pepper.
  3. Add the cooked noodles and peas to the soup mixture and toss to combine.
  4. Transfer the mixture to an 11 x 7-inch baking dish coated with cooking spray.
  5. In a small bowl, toss breadcrumbs with melted butter, then sprinkle evenly over the top of the casserole.
  6. Bake, uncovered, for 25–30 minutes, or until bubbly and heated through.
  7. Note: To make soft breadcrumbs, tear bread into pieces and place in a food processor or blender. Pulse until crumbs form. One slice of bread yields 1/2 to 3/4 cup crumbs.
