Chicago Deep-Dish Sausage Pizza

Notes

  • Yield: not specified in original notes

Ingredients

  • 1 lb bulk Italian pork sausage
  • 1 small bell pepper (any color), chopped (1/2 cup)
  • 1 cup sliced fresh mushrooms
  • 1 can (8 oz) pizza sauce
  • 1 can (13.8 oz) refrigerated classic pizza crust
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese (6 oz)
  • 2 medium plum (Roma) tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/4 cup sliced ripe olives, if desired
  • 2 medium green onions, chopped (2 tbsp)

Instructions

  1. Heat oven to 400 degrees. Spray a 9-inch square pan with cooking spray.
  2. In a 10-inch nonstick skillet, cook the sausage and bell pepper over medium-high heat for 7–9 minutes, stirring frequently, until sausage is no longer pink. Stir in mushrooms and pizza sauce. Keep warm over low heat.
  3. Unroll the dough and place in the pan. Starting at the center, press out dough to edges of pan, pressing at least 1 inch up sides and folding edges under to form a crust.
  4. Sprinkle 1/2 cup of the cheese evenly over the crust. Spoon the hot sausage mixture over the cheese. Top with the remaining 1 cup cheese, tomatoes, and olives.
  5. Bake 15–20 minutes or until crust is golden brown. Sprinkle with green onions. Let stand 5 minutes before cutting.
  6. *Optional: Brown crust a little first before adding sauce.
