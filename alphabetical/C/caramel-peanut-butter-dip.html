Caramel Peanut Butter Dip

Notes

  • Yield: 1 serving (250 Calories)

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp powdered peanut butter
  • 2 tbsp CSE Simply Vanilla Protein Powder
  • 2 tbsp water
  • 1 tsp raw honey
  • 1/2 tbsp OffBeat Sweet Classic Peanut Butter or natural peanut butter
  • Dash of cinnamon
  • 4 g (1 tsp) mini chocolate chips
  • 70 g apple slices

Instructions

  1. Stir the powdered peanut butter, protein powder, and water together in a small bowl until smooth.
  2. Add the honey, peanut butter, and cinnamon. Stir until well combined.
  3. Top with mini chocolate chips and use as a dip for your apple slices.
