Chocolate Chip Muffins

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cup flour
  • 1/3 cup sugar
  • 2 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup chocolate chips
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1/3 cup melted margarine (or applesauce)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400Â°F. Grease muffin cups.
  2. In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking powder and salt. Stir in chocolate chips.
  3. Add milk, egg, and melted margarine (or applesauce). Mix just until moistened. Batter will be lumpy. Do not overbeat.
  4. Spoon batter into greased muffin cups.
  5. Bake for about 18 minutes, or until tops spring back when lightly touched. You may need less time when making 12 smaller muffins; bake about 18 minutes when making 6 larger muffins.
  6. Serve warm.
