Cinnamon Banana Oatmeal

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1 cup oat milk
  • Mashed banana
  • Cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Mix oats, oat milk, mashed banana, and cinnamon in a pot.
  2. Cook over medium heat for about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the oats are tender and creamy.
  3. Top with more cinnamon and sliced bananas, if desired.
