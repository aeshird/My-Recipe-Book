Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough Bites

Ingredients

  • 1 cup natural peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup raw honey
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 serving CSE Simply Vanilla Protein Powder
  • 1 1/2 cups CSE Pancake + Waffle Mix (Buttermilk)
  • Dash of sea salt
  • 30 dark chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Mix the peanut butter, honey, and vanilla extract together.
  2. In a separate bowl, add the protein powder, pancake mix, sea salt, and chocolate chips. Combine with wet mixture.
  3. Using a cookie scoop, scoop into balls. Store in the fridge or freezer.

Notes

  • It may be easier to mix honey, protein powder, and peanut butter first before adding remaining ingredients.
