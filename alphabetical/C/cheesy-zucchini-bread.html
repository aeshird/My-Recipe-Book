Cheesy Zucchini Bread

Notes

  • Yield: 6 servings
  • Prep: 20 min | Cook: 20 min | Total: 40 min

Ingredients

  • 4 cups grated zucchini
  • 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese
  • 1/3 cup parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp garlic parsley salt
  • 1 cup grated cheese of choice (for topping)

Instructions

  1. Squeeze as much juice as you can out of the grated zucchini using your hands or a kitchen towel.
  2. In a bowl, combine the grated zucchini, mozzarella cheese, parmesan cheese, egg, and garlic salt. Stir until everything is well combined.
  3. On a lined baking dish with parchment paper or a silicone baking mat, spread out the zucchini mixture.
  4. Spread to at least 1/2-inch thickness (it will shrink while baking). Bake at 425 degrees for 15 minutes.
  5. Top the baked crust with your choice of cheese. Bake until the cheese melts. Serve while hot.
