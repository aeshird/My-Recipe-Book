Cheesy Lasagna Roll Ups

Notes

  • Yield: 12 servings
  • Prep: 15 min | Cook: 35 min | Total: 50 min

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 container (15 oz) ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese, divided
  • 1/3 cup grated Romano cheese
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp garlic salt
  • 3 tbsp chopped parsley, divided
  • 2 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
  • 1 jar Ragù Homestyle Meat Sauce

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Spread enough pasta sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 pan to lightly coat. Set aside.
  2. Cook lasagna noodles in a large pot of water until al dente, according to package directions.
  3. Meanwhile, in a medium bowl, lightly beat the egg. Add ricotta cheese, pepper, salt, and garlic salt and mix well. Stir in 2/3 cup grated Parmesan, 1/3 cup grated Romano, 2 cups shredded mozzarella, and 2 tbsp chopped parsley.
  4. Lay out cooked lasagna noodles on 2 baking sheets. Evenly spread a thin layer of cheese mixture down the center of each noodle. Spread a spoonful of meat sauce down each noodle, on top of the cheese mixture.
  5. Roll each noodle up, jelly roll–style, and place seam side down in the prepared baking dish. Top the roll-ups with remaining sauce. Sprinkle with remaining 1 cup mozzarella, 1/3 cup grated Parmesan, and 1 tbsp chopped parsley.
  6. Loosely tent with aluminum foil and bake for 35 minutes.
