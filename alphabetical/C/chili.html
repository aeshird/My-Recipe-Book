Chili

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 small green pepper, chopped
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 (15 oz) can tomato sauce
  • 1 (15 oz) can chili beans in chili sauce
  • 1 (14 1/2 oz) can diced tomatoes
  • 2 scant tbsp chili powder
  • Dash basil leaves
  • Tomato juice, as needed (about 6 oz per 1 lb ground beef), or substitute noted below

Instructions

  1. In a large pot or skillet, brown the ground beef with the chopped green pepper and onion. Drain excess fat.
  2. Add the cooked beef mixture to a large pot (if not already in one). Stir in tomato sauce, chili beans, diced tomatoes, chili powder, and basil.
  3. Add about 6 oz tomato juice (per 1 lb ground beef) or more to reach desired consistency.
  4. Bring to a gentle simmer and cook until heated through and flavors meld, stirring occasionally.

Notes

  • Instead of tomato juice, you can use 8 oz tomato sauce plus 1 can (8 oz) water.
  • Alternative method: Brown beef, add remaining ingredients, and simmer in crockpot for about 2 hours.
