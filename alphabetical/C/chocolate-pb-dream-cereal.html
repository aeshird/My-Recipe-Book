Chocolate PB Dream Cereal

Notes

  • Yield: 1 serving (395 Calories)

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup Peanut Butter Panda Puffs cereal
  • 30 g banana, sliced
  • 1/2 tbsp all-natural peanut butter
  • 2 tsp mini chocolate chips
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 scoop CSE Brownie Batter or Simply Vanilla protein powder

Instructions

  1. Add cereal to a bowl. Top with bananas, peanut butter, and mini chocolate chips.
  2. Add almond milk and protein powder to a shaker cup. Shake until smooth, then pour over cereal.
