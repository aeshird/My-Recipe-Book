Chocolate Chip Banana Bread

Ingredients

  • 2 cups unbleached all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/8 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1/8 tsp ground cloves
  • 2 large eggs, beaten lightly
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 1/2 cups very ripe bananas, mashed well (about 3 medium)
  • 1/4 cup plain yogurt
  • 6 tbsp unsalted butter, melted and cooled
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 6–8 oz milk chocolate or semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1–2 oz chocolate chips for sprinkling on top (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Place rack in middle of oven. Prepare a 9x5 inch loaf pan by buttering and flouring the base if nonstick (butter sides too if not nonstick), or use nonstick baking spray.
  2. In a small mixing bowl, combine flour, baking soda, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, nutmeg, and cloves. Whisk to combine. Reserve 1/2 tbsp of this flour mixture and toss it with 6–8 oz chocolate chips.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together eggs and sugar. Add mashed bananas, yogurt, melted butter, and vanilla, whisking until thoroughly combined.
  4. Gently fold the wet and dry ingredients together just until the dry ingredients are moistened. Batter should be thick and chunky, but without visible streaks of flour.
  5. Fold in the flour-coated chocolate chips.
  6. Pour batter into prepared loaf pan and smooth the top. Sprinkle with 1–2 oz additional chocolate chips if desired.
  7. Bake until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean or with just a few moist crumbs (time will depend on your oven; typically around 55–65 minutes).
  8. Cool in pan for about 10–15 minutes, then remove to a wire rack to cool completely before slicing.
