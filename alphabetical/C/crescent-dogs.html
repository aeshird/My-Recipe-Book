Crescent Dogs

Notes

  • Yield: 8 servings
  • Prep: 10 min | Total: 25 min

Ingredients

  • 8 hot dogs
  • 4 slices (3/4 oz each) American cheese, each cut into 6 strips
  • 1 can (8 oz) refrigerated Pillsbury Original Crescent Rolls (8 count)

Instructions

  1. Heat oven to 375°F.
  2. Slice each hot dog lengthwise to within 1/2 inch of the ends. Insert 3 strips of cheese into each slit.
  3. Separate crescent dough into 8 triangles.
  4. Wrap one dough triangle around each hot dog, leaving cheese side up. Place on an ungreased cookie sheet, cheese side up.
  5. Bake for 12–15 minutes, or until golden brown.
