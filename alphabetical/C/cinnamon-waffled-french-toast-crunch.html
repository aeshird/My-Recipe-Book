Cinnamon Waffled French Toast Crunch

Notes

  • Yield: 1 serving (340 Calories)

Ingredients

  • 4 tbsp liquid egg whites
  • 2 tbsp fat-free milk
  • 1 tbsp CSE Simply Vanilla Protein Powder
  • Dash vanilla
  • Dash cinnamon
  • 2 slices Harperâ€™s Bran Bread
  • 1 tbsp OffBeat Cinnamon Bun Butter
  • 1/2 tbsp raw honey

Instructions

  1. Heat waffle iron.
  2. Add the egg whites, milk, protein powder, vanilla, and cinnamon to a shallow dish. Whisk all ingredients together until well combined.
  3. Dip each slice of bread into the egg mixture, coating each side evenly. Place the slices one at a time onto a greased waffle iron and cook until golden and crisp.
  4. Top with Cinnamon Bun Butter and drizzle with honey.
