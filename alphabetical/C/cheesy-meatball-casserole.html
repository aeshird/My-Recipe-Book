Cheesy Meatball Casserole

Notes

  • Yield: 6 servings
  • Prep: 10 min | Cook: 25 min | Total: 35 min

Ingredients

  • 2 lb (900 g) ground turkey
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella
  • 1/2 cup grated or shredded Parmesan
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tsp onion powder
  • 2 tsp fresh minced garlic
  • 1/2 tsp Italian seasoning blend
  • 1/2 tsp fresh cracked black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp Cayenne pepper, optional
  • 1/2 tsp crushed red chili pepper flakes, optional
  • 1 cup Marinara sauce
  • 1 cup shredded cheese of your choice for topping (Mozzarella, Provolone, Monterrey Jack, Cheddar, etc.)
  • Fresh basil or parsley, for topping

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Lightly spray a casserole dish with cooking spray.
  2. In a large bowl, combine ground turkey, mozzarella, Parmesan, egg, onion powder, garlic, Italian seasoning, Cayenne, and crushed chili pepper flakes. Mix just until combined.
  3. Use a small cookie scoop to form meatballs all the same size and arrange them in the casserole dish.
  4. Bake for 15–20 minutes, or until fully cooked. Remove the casserole from the oven and carefully drain any grease.
  5. Top with Marinara sauce and shredded cheese. Return to the oven and bake an additional 5–10 minutes, until the cheese has fully melted.
  6. Garnish with fresh basil or parsley. Serve with a side salad or over zucchini noodles, if desired.
