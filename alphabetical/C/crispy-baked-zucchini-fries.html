Crispy Baked Zucchini Fries

Notes

  • Yield: 4 servings
  • Prep: 15 min | Cook: 20 min | Total: 35 min

Ingredients

  • 2 medium zucchini
  • 2 egg whites or 1 egg, whisked
  • 1/2 cup almond flour or almond meal
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1 tsp sea salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Coat a baking sheet with cooking spray or line with parchment paper.
  2. In a small bowl, mix together almond flour, Parmesan cheese, Italian seasoning, and salt.
  3. Slice each zucchini in half lengthwise, then slice each half in half widthwise. Slice each piece into 4–6 fries, keeping them roughly the same size.
  4. Dip a zucchini piece into the egg wash and shake off any excess. Dredge in the almond flour-Parmesan mixture, coating well, and place on the prepared baking sheet. Repeat with remaining zucchini pieces.
  5. Bake for 10 minutes, then flip each fry and return to the oven. Bake for another 10 minutes, or until zucchini fries are crisp and golden brown.
  6. Remove from the oven and serve immediately. They are delicious on their own, but feel free to pair with your favorite dipping sauce. For a dairy-free option, you can swap nutritional yeast for the cheese.
