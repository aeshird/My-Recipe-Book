Creamy Dark Chocolate Protein Smoothie

Ingredients

  • 2 scoops chocolate protein powder
  • 8 oz coconut almond milk
  • 1 tsp cocoa powder
  • 1 packet stevia
  • 6 ice cubes

Instructions

  1. Combine all ingredients in a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
