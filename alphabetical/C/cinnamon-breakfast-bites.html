Cinnamon Breakfast Bites

Notes

  • Yield: 6 servings (about 2 1/2 dozen bites)
  • Bake: 15–18 minutes at 425°F

Ingredients

  • 1 1/3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup crisp rice cereal, coarsely crushed
  • 2 tbsp plus 1/2 cup sugar, divided
  • 3 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 cup butter-flavored shortening
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter or margarine, melted

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F.
  2. In a bowl, combine the flour, crushed cereal, 2 tablespoons sugar, baking powder, and salt. Cut in shortening until mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  3. Stir in milk just until moistened. Shape dough into 1-inch balls.
  4. In a small bowl, combine cinnamon and remaining 1/2 cup sugar. Place melted butter in another small bowl.
  5. Dip each ball into melted butter, then roll in cinnamon-sugar.
  6. Arrange the coated balls in a single layer in an 8-inch round baking pan.
  7. Bake for 15–18 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
