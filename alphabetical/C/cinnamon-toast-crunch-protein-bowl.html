Cinnamon Toast Crunch Protein Bowl

Notes

  • Yield: 1 serving (379 Calories)

Ingredients

  • 1 cup nonfat plain Greek yogurt
  • 1 tbsp CSE Simply Vanilla Protein Powder
  • Vanilla stevia drops (optional)
  • 60 g banana, sliced
  • 1/4 cup strawberries, sliced
  • 1/4 cup Cinnamon Toast Crunch cereal
  • 1 tbsp all-natural peanut butter
  • 1 tsp mini chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Add Greek yogurt to a bowl. Stir in protein powder and stevia drops, if using, for sweetness. Top with bananas, strawberries, and cereal.
  2. Melt peanut butter in the microwave for about 30 seconds. Drizzle over the top of the fruit and sprinkle chocolate chips on top.
