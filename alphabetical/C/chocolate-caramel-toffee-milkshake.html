Chocolate Caramel Toffee Milkshake

Notes

  • Yield: 1 serving (230 Calories)

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsweetened vanilla almond milk
  • 1 serving (33 g) CSE Caramel Toffee Protein Powder
  • 1 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 1/4 tsp xanthan gum
  • 10â€“12 ice cubes (about 160 g)

Toppings

  • 2 tbsp spray whipped cream topping
  • 2 tsp mini chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Add all shake ingredients to a high-powered blender and blend on high until smooth.
  2. Pour into a cup and top with whipped cream and chocolate chips.
  3. Enjoy with a spoon; it should be thick and creamy.
