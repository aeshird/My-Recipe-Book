Caprese Chicken Salad

Notes

  • Yield: 4 servings (355 Calories per serving)

Ingredients

  • 10 oz chicken breast, cooked and sliced
  • 8 cups spring salad mix
  • 1/2 cup quinoa, cooked
  • 2 oz fresh mozzarella pearls
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Pinch of fresh basil leaves
  • 4 medium sized apples

Balsamic Vinaigrette Dressing

  • 1/2 tsp garlic, minced
  • 1/2 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1/2 tsp fresh lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Sea salt and pepper, to taste
  • Stevia in the raw, to taste

Instructions

  1. Place all dressing ingredients into a blender. Blend until smooth. Place dressing on a food scale and divide the total weight by 4 to get your individual serving size.
  2. For each serving, place 2 cups spring salad mix on a plate and layer with 2.5 oz chicken, 1 tbsp cooked quinoa, 1/2 oz mozzarella pearls, 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, and a pinch of fresh basil leaves.
  3. Drizzle with the Balsamic Dressing. Enjoy one sliced apple on the side.
