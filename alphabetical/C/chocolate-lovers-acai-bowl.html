Chocolate Lover's Acai Bowl

Notes

  • Yield: 1 serving (335 Calories)

Ingredients

  • 1 frozen unsweetened acai pack (e.g., Sambazon)
  • 40 g frozen bananas
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened almond & coconut milk
  • 1 serving CSE Brownie Batter Protein
  • 1 tbsp special dark cocoa powder
  • 4â€“6 ice cubes

Toppings

  • 20 g banana, sliced
  • 20 g strawberries, sliced
  • 1 tbsp sliced almonds
  • 1 tbsp granola

Instructions

  1. Place frozen acai in a blender and pulse until broken up. Scrape down the sides of the blender.
  2. Add the almond & coconut milk, frozen bananas, protein powder, cocoa powder and ice. Blend until smooth and thick.
  3. Pour into a bowl and top with bananas, strawberries, almonds and granola.
