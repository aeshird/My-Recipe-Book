Chunky Coco-Monkey Shake

Notes

  • Yield: 1 serving (325 Calories)

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 21 g OffBeat Monkey Business Butter
  • 1.5 servings CSE Coconut Cream Protein Powder
  • 125 g ice cubes

Instructions

  1. Add all of the ingredients to a high-powered blender. Blend on high until smooth.
  2. Pour into a cup and enjoy!
