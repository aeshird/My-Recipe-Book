Chicken & Broccoli Alfredo

Notes

  • Yield and times not specified in original notes.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz rotini pasta
  • 12 oz broccoli florets
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 boneless, skinless thin-sliced chicken breasts
  • Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup chicken broth
  • 3/4 cup milk, or more as needed
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 cup freshly grated Parmesan
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh parsley leaves

Instructions

  1. In a large pot of boiling salted water, cook pasta according to package instructions. During the last 2 minutes of cooking time, add the broccoli. Drain well.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Season chicken breasts with salt and pepper. Add to skillet and cook, flipping once, until cooked through, about 3–4 minutes per side. Let cool slightly, then dice into bite-size pieces.
  3. In the same skillet, melt butter over medium heat. Whisk in flour and cook until lightly browned, about 1 minute.
  4. Gradually whisk in chicken broth and milk, cooking and whisking constantly until incorporated, about 1–2 minutes. Stir in heavy cream and garlic powder and continue to cook until slightly thickened, about 1–2 minutes.
  5. Stir in Parmesan until well combined. If the sauce is too thick, add more milk as needed.
  6. Add the pasta, broccoli, and chicken to the skillet. Toss gently to coat everything in the sauce. Sprinkle with fresh parsley and serve warm.
