Cinnamon Sugar Donut Muffins

Notes

  • Yield: 10 servings (220 Calories each)
  • Prep: 10 min | Cook: 20 min | Total: 30 min

Ingredients (Muffins)

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/3 cup butter, melted and cooled

Ingredients (Topping)

  • 1/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 5 tbsp butter, melted

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Grease muffin cups or line with paper liners. Set aside.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, and nutmeg. Set aside.
  3. In a small bowl, beat the egg. Add the milk, vanilla extract, and melted butter and mix well to combine.
  4. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and stir just until moistened, being careful not to overmix.
  5. Spoon batter into muffin cups, filling each about 1/2 to 3/4 full. Bake for 20–25 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Allow muffins to cool for 5 minutes before removing from pan.
  6. For the topping, mix the cinnamon and sugar together in a small bowl. Melt the 5 tablespoons of butter in another small bowl.
  7. Dip the tops of the muffins into the melted butter, then roll in the cinnamon-sugar mixture to coat.
