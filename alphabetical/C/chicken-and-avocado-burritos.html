Chicken & Avocado Burritos

Notes

  • Yield: 4 servings (519 Calories)
  • Prep: 15 min | Cook: 5 min | Total: 20 min

Ingredients

  • 4 burrito-sized tortillas (corn tortillas for gluten-free), warmed
  • 1 lb cooked chicken, sliced or shredded
  • 1 large avocado, diced
  • 1 cup Monterey Jack cheese, shredded
  • 1/4 cup salsa verde
  • 1/4 cup sour cream or Greek yogurt
  • 2 tbsp cilantro, chopped

Instructions

  1. Assemble the burritos by layering chicken, avocado, cheese, salsa verde, sour cream or Greek yogurt, and cilantro in the warmed tortillas.
  2. Roll up each burrito, tucking in the sides as you go. Optionally, toast the burritos in a skillet or on a griddle until lightly browned.
  3. Serve warm and enjoy!

Notes

  • You can use taco lime grilled chicken, cilantro lime grilled chicken, or tequila lime grilled chicken instead of plain chicken.
