Cinnamon Bread (From Ruby)

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 scant tbsp melted butter
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3/4 tsp cinnamon
  • 2 tsp baking powder

Topping

  • 2 tbsp white sugar
  • 2 tbsp plus 2 tsp brown sugar
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Grease an 8 x 8-inch baking pan.
  2. Beat the egg in a bowl. Add the 1/2 cup sugar, melted butter, and milk and mix well.
  3. In a separate bowl, sift together flour, salt, 3/4 tsp cinnamon, and baking powder.
  4. Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and mix just until combined.
  5. Pour batter into the greased 8 x 8-inch baking pan.
  6. In a small bowl, combine the topping ingredients (white sugar, brown sugar, and 1/2 tsp cinnamon).
  7. Sprinkle the topping mixture evenly over the batter.
  8. Bake at 350°F for about 15 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  9. Note: Topping may also be added to the other ingredients and then sprinkled on top for more flavor.
