Cheesy Garlic Burgers with Lemon Butter Zucchini Noodles

Notes

  • Yield: 4 servings
  • Prep: 10 min | Cook: 20 min

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 lb (650 g) lean ground beef
  • 5 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley, divided
  • 1 tsp crumbled beef bouillon cube
  • Crushed red chili pepper flakes, optional
  • 1 tbsp steak seasoning
  • 4 medium zucchini, spiralized or julienned
  • 4 cheese slices (of your choosing)
  • 2 tbsp butter + 2 tbsp butter (divided)
  • 1 tbsp hot sauce
  • Salt and fresh cracked pepper
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • Juice of 1/2 lemon + lemon slices, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Add ground beef to a medium bowl, breaking it up as you do.
  2. Sprinkle with steak seasoning, red chili pepper flakes (if using), fresh cracked black pepper, half of the minced garlic, bouillon cube, and half of the parsley.
  3. Mix with a fork or by hand until the ingredients are just combined. Form into 4 patties.
  4. Cook the burgers in a skillet with a bit of butter until cooked to your liking. Top each patty with a slice of cheese to melt.
  5. In the same pan, add remaining butter, hot sauce, Italian seasoning, remaining garlic, and lemon juice. Toss in the zucchini noodles and cook briefly until just tender. Season to taste.
  6. Serve each cheesy burger over a bed of lemon butter zucchini noodles. Garnish with remaining parsley and lemon slices.
