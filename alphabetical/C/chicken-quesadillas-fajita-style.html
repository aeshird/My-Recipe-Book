Chicken Quesadillas (Fajita Style)

Notes

  • Yield: 6 servings
  • Prep: 20 min | Total: 45 min

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 lb boneless skinless chicken breasts, diced into 1/2-inch cubes
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 4 tsp olive oil, divided
  • 2 cups chopped bell pepper (about 2 peppers)
  • 2/3 cup chopped red onion
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp fresh lime juice
  • 6 (10-inch, burrito-size) flour tortillas
  • 3 tbsp butter, melted
  • 6 oz cheddar cheese, shredded (1 1/2 cups)
  • 6 oz Monterey Jack cheese, shredded (1 1/2 cups)
  • Guacamole, sour cream, pico de gallo, or salsa (for serving, optional)

Instructions

  1. Toss chicken with chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper (about 3/4 tsp salt and 1/2 tsp pepper).
  2. Heat 2 tsp olive oil in a 12-inch non-stick skillet over medium-high heat. Add chicken and cook, turning occasionally, until fully cooked through, about 6 minutes. Transfer to a medium mixing bowl.
  3. Heat remaining 2 tsp oil in the same skillet. Add bell pepper and red onion and saut√© until tender, about 6 minutes, adding the garlic during the last 1 minute. Transfer to the bowl with the chicken, pour in lime juice, and toss.
  4. Brush the top side of each tortilla with melted butter. Wipe the skillet clean with paper towels and heat over medium heat. Toss the cheeses together in a bowl.
  5. Transfer one tortilla, buttered side down, to the skillet. Working quickly, sprinkle 1/2 cup cheese evenly over one half of the tortilla, then sprinkle with about 3/4 cup of the chicken mixture. Fold the tortilla in half over the filling.
  6. Cook until the bottom is golden and crisp, then flip and cook the other side until the cheese is melted. Repeat with remaining tortillas, cheese, and filling. Slice and serve with desired toppings.
