Caprese Avocado Toast

Notes

  • Yield: 2 servings
  • Prep: 10 min | Total: 10 min

Ingredients

  • 2 slices 100% whole-wheat bread
  • 1 avocado
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1/4 tsp ground pepper
  • 2 slices fresh mozzarella cheese
  • 4 slices tomato
  • 4 leaves basil, torn
  • 2 tsp balsamic vinegar

Instructions

  1. Toast bread until lightly browned, about 2â€“3 minutes.
  2. Mash avocado in a small bowl. Stir in lemon juice and pepper.
  3. Spread the avocado mixture on the toast. Top with mozzarella, tomato, and basil.
  4. Drizzle with balsamic vinegar and serve.
