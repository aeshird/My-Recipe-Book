Chocolate Chip Cookies

Notes

  • Yield: About 3 dozen cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup grass-fed butter
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup light brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla (not imitation)
  • 3 cups flour
  • 3/4 tsp sea salt
  • 3/4 tsp baking soda
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Take butter out of the fridge an hour before using so it is cold, but soft.
  2. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  3. Cream together the butter, sugar and brown sugar. Beat in the eggs and vanilla until smooth.
  4. In a separate bowl, combine the flour, sea salt and baking soda.
  5. Add the dry mixture to the wet mixture and mix on low until just combined. Add the chocolate chips and fold into the dough.
  6. Drop dough onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper by heaping tablespoons. Bake for 9–11 minutes. Makes about 3 dozen cookies.
