Cauliflower Pizza Crust

Notes

  • Yield: 4 servings
  • Prep: 15 min | Cook: 35 min | Total: 50 min

Crust Ingredients

  • 2 cups riced cauliflower (about 1/2 head)
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 cup part-skim shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Olive oil spray or 1 tsp olive oil, for greasing

Toppings

  • 1/3 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup part-skim shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil
  • Crushed red pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. Line a baking sheet or pizza stone with parchment paper. Grease the parchment with olive oil spray or rub on regular olive oil.
  3. If using whole cauliflower, remove the stems and leaves and chop the florets into chunks. Add to a food processor or blender and pulse just until the texture is similar to rice (or use pre-riced cauliflower).
  4. Sauté cauliflower “rice” in a non-stick skillet over medium heat until translucent, about 6–8 minutes. Let cool slightly.
  5. In a bowl, mix the cooked cauliflower, minced garlic, 1 cup mozzarella, egg, basil, and oregano until well combined.
  6. Spread the mixture onto the prepared baking sheet or pizza stone, forming a thin, even crust.
  7. Bake until the crust is set and starting to turn golden.
  8. Top with pizza sauce, remaining mozzarella, fresh basil, and crushed red pepper. Return to the oven and bake until cheese is melted and bubbly.
