Round Steak Stroganoff

Notes

  • Yield: 7 servings
  • Prep: 15 min
  • Total: 7 hr 15 min
  • Cooker: 2â€“3 quart slow cooker

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 lb boneless beef round steak (1/2 to 3/4 in. thick), trimmed of fat, cut into bite-size strips
  • 2 tbsp onion soup mix (from 1 oz package)
  • 1 jar (4.5 oz) sliced mushrooms, drained
  • 1 can (10 3/4 oz) condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 1/2 cups uncooked regular long-grain white rice
  • 3 cups water
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh chives, optional

Instructions

  1. Spray a 2- to 3-quart slow cooker with cooking spray.
  2. In the cooker, mix beef strips and onion soup mix until beef is evenly coated.
  3. Top with sliced mushrooms and condensed cream of mushroom soup.
  4. Cover and cook on low heat setting for 7 to 8 hours, or until beef is tender.
  5. About 30 minutes before serving, cook rice in water as directed on package.
  6. Just before serving, stir sour cream into beef mixture. Sprinkle with chives, if desired.
  7. Serve the beef mixture over hot cooked rice.
