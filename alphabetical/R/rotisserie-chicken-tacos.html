Rotisserie Chicken Tacos

Notes

  • Yield: 4 servings (345 Calories each)

Ingredients

  • 10 oz rotisserie chicken, shredded
  • 1â€“2 tbsp taco seasoning
  • 8 corn tortillas
  • 4 tbsp low-fat shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 4 clementine oranges

Toppings (Per Serving)

  • 1/2 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 tbsp guacamole
  • 2 tbsp pico de gallo
  • 1 tbsp cashew sour cream (see food guide)
  • Juice of 1/2 lime
  • Cilantro, to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to high broil.
  2. In a bowl, combine shredded chicken and taco seasoning.
  3. Lay tortillas out on a baking pan. For each serving, fill 2 tortillas with 2.5 oz chicken and 1 tbsp mozzarella cheese.
  4. Place in the oven and broil for about 3 minutes, or until cheese is melted and tortillas are lightly crisp.
  5. Remove from oven and add lettuce, guacamole, pico de gallo, cashew sour cream, lime juice, and cilantro to taste.
  6. Serve with 1 clementine on the side for each serving.
