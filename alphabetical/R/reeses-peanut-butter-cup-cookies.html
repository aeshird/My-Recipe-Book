Reese’s Peanut Butter Cup Cookies

Notes

  • Yield: 7 dozen cookies
  • Oven: 350°F

Ingredients

  • 1 cup margarine, softened
  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 1/2 cups flour
  • 2 bags (13 oz each) Reese’s miniature peanut butter cups
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (176°C).
  2. In a large bowl, mix margarine, peanut butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until well combined. Add flour and mix again.
  3. Stir in eggs, baking soda, vanilla, and salt. Mix well to form a soft dough.
  4. Form dough into balls about the size of a walnut and place in ungreased 1 1/2-inch muffin tins.
  5. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes, or until lightly browned.
  6. Remove from oven and immediately press one miniature peanut butter cup into the center of each cookie.
  7. Cool for a few minutes before removing cookies from tins. Cool completely on a wire rack.
