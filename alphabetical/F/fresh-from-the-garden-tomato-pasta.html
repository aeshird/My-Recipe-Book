Fresh From the Garden Tomato Pasta

Notes

  • Yield: 4 servings
  • Prep: 10 min | Total: 20 min

Ingredients

  • 3 cups rotini pasta, uncooked
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1/4 cup Kraft Special Collection Balsamic Vinaigrette Dressing
  • 4 cups coarsely chopped tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh basil
  • 1/2 cup Kraft 100% Grated Parmesan Cheese

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta as directed on package. Drain and keep warm.
  2. In a large nonstick skillet, cook garlic and onion in the balsamic vinaigrette dressing for about 2 minutes, or until softened.
  3. Add tomatoes and cook an additional 2 minutes.
  4. In a large bowl, toss the warm pasta with the tomato mixture, fresh basil, and Parmesan cheese until well combined.
  5. Serve warm. Optionally, serve with grilled chicken.
