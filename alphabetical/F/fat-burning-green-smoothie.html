Fat Burning Green Smoothie

Ingredients

  • Spinach
  • Banana
  • Pineapple
  • Chia seeds
  • Ginger
  • Almond milk

Instructions

  1. Add spinach, banana, pineapple, chia seeds, ginger, and almond milk to a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth, adjusting the amount of almond milk to reach your desired consistency.
  3. Serve immediately.
