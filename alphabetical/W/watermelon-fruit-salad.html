Watermelon Fruit Salad

Notes

  • Yield: 6 servings
  • Cook Time: 10 minutes

Salad Ingredients

  • 4 cups watermelon, cubed into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 1/2 cups strawberries, sliced into rounds
  • 1 1/2 cups raspberries
  • 1 1/2 cups cherries, pitted and halved

Dressing Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 1 1/2 tsp lime zest
  • 2 tbsp agave syrup
  • 1 tbsp fresh mint, finely chopped

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the agave, lime juice, lime zest, and chopped mint until well combined.
  2. Prep the watermelon and berries and place them in a large mixing bowl.
  3. Drizzle the dressing over the fruit and lightly toss until everything is coated.
  4. Garnish with additional torn mint leaves, if desired, and serve.
