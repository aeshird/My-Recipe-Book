Keto Sheet Pan Pizza

Notes

  • Yield: 8 servings
  • Prep: 30 min | Cook: 30 min | Total: 1 hr

Dough

  • 1/2 lb sweet Italian sausage, removed from casings
  • 2 tbsp cream cheese
  • 1 cup almond flour
  • 1/4 cup coconut flour
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 large eggs

Toppings

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 1 medium bell pepper, sliced (any color)
  • 8 oz baby spinach
  • 2 tbsp fresh garlic, pressed
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce (no added sugar)
  • 1/2 cup shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
  2. In a medium bowl, mix the uncooked sausage, cream cheese, almond flour, coconut flour, 1 1/2 cups mozzarella, Parmesan, and eggs until well combined.
  3. Spray a quarter sheet pan (13 x 9-inch) with nonstick spray. Press the mixture evenly into the pan, creating a uniform crust with a slight edge around the sides.
  4. If the mixture is very soft, refrigerate the filled pan for 30 minutes to firm up the crust before baking.
  5. Bake the crust for about 12 minutes, turning the pan halfway through baking, until just set and lightly browned.
  6. While the crust bakes, heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add sliced onion and bell pepper and sauté until softened, about 5–7 minutes.
  7. Add the spinach and garlic to the skillet and cook until the spinach is wilted and any excess moisture has evaporated. Remove from heat.
  8. Remove the crust from the oven. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the crust.
  9. Top with the sautéed vegetable mixture, then sprinkle with shredded cheddar and the remaining 2 cups mozzarella cheese.
  10. Return the pan to the oven and bake for 15–18 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly and the edges are golden brown.
  11. Let cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
