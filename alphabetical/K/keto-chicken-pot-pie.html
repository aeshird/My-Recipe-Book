Keto Chicken Pot Pie

Notes

  • Yield: 8 servings
  • Prep: 5 min | Cook: 40 min | Rest: 10 min | Total: 55 min

Fat Head Crust

  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 tbsp cream cheese
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 1 cup super fine almond flour

Filling

  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp garlic, chopped
  • 1/2 cup onion, finely chopped
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1/4 tsp xanthan gum
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 1/4 cups frozen mixed vegetables
  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F. Grease an 8-inch pie pan and set aside.
  2. Make the fat head crust: In a large microwave-safe bowl, combine the mozzarella cheese and cream cheese. Microwave on high for 45 seconds. Stir, then microwave for 30 more seconds. Stir until fully combined and soft.
  3. Stir in the almond flour as well as you can, then add the beaten egg. Using a fork, mix until mostly combined.
  4. Dust your hands (or the outside of the dough) lightly with almond flour or wet your hands, then knead the dough gently until smooth and fully combined.
  5. Divide the dough in half. Press one half evenly into the bottom and up the sides of the prepared pie pan to form the bottom crust.
  6. Bake the bottom crust for about 8–10 minutes, just until it is set and starting to lightly brown. Remove from the oven and let cool slightly while you prepare the filling.
  7. Make the filling: In a skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the chopped garlic and onion and sauté until softened, 3–4 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
  8. Sprinkle in the xanthan gum and stir, cooking for 1 minute. Slowly add the heavy cream while stirring, and cook until the mixture has thickened slightly.
  9. Stir in the frozen mixed vegetables and shredded chicken. Cook for 3–4 minutes, until heated through. Remove from heat and stir in the shredded cheddar cheese.
  10. Pour the filling into the pre-baked crust and spread evenly.
  11. Roll or press the remaining dough between two sheets of parchment into a circle large enough to cover the pie. Place over the filling and pinch the edges to seal. Cut a few slits in the top to vent steam.
  12. Bake for 20–25 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbly. Let rest for about 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
