Keto Chicken Stir Fry (Low Carb)

Notes

  • Prep: 10 min | Cook: 15 min | Total: 25 min

For the Chicken

  • 1 lb chicken breasts, cut into thin slices
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 cup onion, diced
  • 1/2 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 cups fresh broccoli florets
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 2 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 cup mushrooms (optional)

For the Sauce/Marinade

  • 4 tbsp coconut aminos or gluten-free soy sauce
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1-inch piece ginger, grated
  • 1 tbsp white wine vinegar
  • Mild all-purpose seasoning, to taste

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine coconut aminos (or soy sauce), minced garlic, grated ginger, white wine vinegar, and all-purpose seasoning. Stir well.
  2. Add the sliced chicken to the bowl and toss until evenly coated. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to marinate.
  3. Heat a large wok over medium heat. Add 1 tbsp olive oil. Once hot, add the diced onion and sauté for 2–3 minutes until softened.
  4. Add the broccoli florets and chopped red bell pepper. Cook for 2–3 minutes until tender-crisp. Add mushrooms, if using, and cook another 2 minutes.
  5. Reduce heat to low and transfer the vegetables to a bowl; cover to keep warm.
  6. Add the remaining 1 tbsp olive oil to the wok. Remove the chicken from the marinade, reserving the marinade. Add the chicken to the wok and cook over high heat, 3–4 minutes per side, until fully cooked and browned in spots.
  7. Return the vegetables to the wok. Pour in the reserved marinade, sesame oil, and red pepper flakes. Toss everything together and cook for 2–3 more minutes, until the sauce is heated through and slightly thickened.
  8. Taste and adjust seasoning if needed. Serve hot.
