Vegetable Dip

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Hellman’s dressing
  • 2 cups sour cream
  • 2 tsp dill weed
  • 2 tsp minced onion
  • 2 tsp garlic salt
  • 2 tsp parsley flakes (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the Hellman’s dressing, sour cream, dill weed, minced onion, garlic salt, and parsley flakes (if using).
  2. Mix until all ingredients are well incorporated and the dip is smooth.
  3. Cover and refrigerate until ready to serve.
  4. If using garlic powder instead of garlic salt, use 1 tsp garlic powder and add salt to taste.
