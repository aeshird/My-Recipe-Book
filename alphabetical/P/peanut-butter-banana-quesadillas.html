Peanut Butter Banana Quesadillas

Notes

  • Prep: 5 min
  • Cook: 5 min
  • Total: 10 min

Ingredients

  • 1 8-inch flour tortilla
  • 2 tbsp peanut butter
  • 1/2 medium banana, sliced
  • 1 tbsp chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Spread the peanut butter evenly over the surface of the tortilla.
  2. Thinly slice the banana, then lay the slices over one half of the tortilla.
  3. Sprinkle the chocolate chips over the same half of the tortilla. Fold the tortilla over to cover the banana and chocolate.
  4. Cook the quesadilla in a skillet over medium-low heat until golden brown and crispy on both sides and the chocolate and banana slices are melty inside.
