Potatoes & Green Beans

Notes

  • Yield: 4 servings (245 Calories)
  • Prep: 10 min
  • Cook: 35 min
  • Total: 45 min

Main

  • 1 1/2 lbs potatoes
  • 1 1/2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/3 tsp pepper
  • 1/2 tbsp taco seasoning
  • 1/2 tbsp oregano

For Green Beans

  • 1 lb green beans
  • 1/2 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Peel the potatoes and cut them into medium-sized cubes. Place the cubes into a mixing bowl and add the olive oil, taco seasoning, oregano, salt, and pepper. Mix everything well to coat the potatoes.
  2. Transfer the potatoes to a large baking sheet and spread them out evenly so they do not touch. Bake at 400°F (200°C) for 15 minutes.
  3. Meanwhile, add the green beans to the same mixing bowl (no need to wash it). Add the olive oil, garlic, and salt, then stir everything together.
  4. Remove the potatoes from the oven and add the green beans to the baking sheet. Mix everything gently, then return the baking sheet to the oven and bake for another 20 to 30 minutes, or until the beans and potatoes are cooked through. Serve warm.
