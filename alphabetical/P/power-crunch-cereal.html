Power Crunch Cereal

Notes

  • Yield: 1 serving (410 Calories)

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 scoops CSE Simply Vanilla Protein Powder
  • 1/2 cup low-sugar granola
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • Cinnamon, to taste
  • 2 1/2 tbsp sliced almonds
  • 40 g banana, sliced
  • Stevia, to taste (optional)

Instructions

  1. Blend the almond milk and 1/4 cup water with the protein powder until smooth. Add cinnamon, almonds, and stevia to taste and mix well.
  2. Pour the mixture over the granola in a bowl, top with sliced banana, and enjoy.
