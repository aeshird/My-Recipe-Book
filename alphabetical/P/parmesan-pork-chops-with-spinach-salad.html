Parmesan Pork Chops with Spinach Salad

Notes

  • Yield: 4 servings (4 cups salad)

Ingredients

  • 3 medium tomatoes, seeded and chopped
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 small garlic clove, minced
  • 1/2 tsp salt, divided
  • 1/8 tsp black pepper, plus more to taste
  • 2 egg whites
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 cup dry breadcrumbs
  • 3 tbsp grated Parmesan cheese
  • 4 thin boneless pork loin chops (1/2 in. thick and 3 oz each)
  • 4 cups fresh baby spinach

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the tomatoes, olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, 1/4 tsp salt, and 1/8 tsp pepper. Toss to combine and set aside.
  2. In a shallow bowl, whisk together the egg whites, Dijon mustard, oregano, and the remaining salt and pepper until blended.
  3. In another shallow bowl, mix the breadcrumbs with the Parmesan cheese.
  4. Dip each pork chop in the egg mixture, then coat in the breadcrumb mixture, pressing lightly to adhere.
  5. Place a large nonstick skillet coated with cooking spray over medium heat. Add the pork chops and cook 2 to 3 minutes on each side, or until golden brown and the pork is tender and cooked through.
  6. Add the spinach to the tomato mixture and toss to combine. Serve the spinach salad alongside the pork chops.
