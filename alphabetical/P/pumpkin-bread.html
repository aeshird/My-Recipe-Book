Pumpkin Bread

Notes

  • Makes 3 loaves
  • Bake: 60 min

Ingredients

  • 3 1/3 cups flour
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp nutmeg
  • 3 cups sugar
  • 1 cup Mazola oil (or other vegetable oil)
  • 4 eggs
  • 2/3 cup water
  • 2 cups pumpkin (from a 15 oz can)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (176°C). Grease and flour 3 loaf pans.
  2. Sift together the flour, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, and nutmeg in a large bowl.
  3. Make a well in the center of the dry ingredients and add the sugar, oil, eggs, water, and pumpkin.
  4. Mix until the batter is smooth and all ingredients are fully incorporated.
  5. Divide the batter evenly among the prepared loaf pans.
  6. Bake at 350°F for 1 hour, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a loaf comes out clean.
  7. Cool on a wire rack, then turn out of the pans and allow to cool completely before slicing.
