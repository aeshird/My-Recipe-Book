Peanut Butter Banana Smoothie Bowl

Notes

  • Yield: 1 serving
  • Total: 5 min

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe bananas, peeled and frozen
  • 2 tbsp creamy peanut butter
  • 2 tbsp flaxseed meal (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract (optional)
  • 1/4 cup nondairy milk (such as almond or coconut)

Instructions

  1. Add all ingredients to a high-powered blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy, adjusting the amount of liquid as needed. Use as little liquid as possible for a thicker smoothie bowl.
  3. Transfer the smoothie to a bowl. Add any desired toppings and enjoy with a spoon.
  4. Recipe note: Be sure to use frozen ripe bananas. Peel, slice, and freeze in a sealed container or bag; they will keep fresh for at least 1 month in the freezer.
