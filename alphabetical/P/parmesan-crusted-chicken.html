Parmesan Crusted Chicken

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 1/2 cup mayo
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese (can)
  • 4 tsp. italian bread crumbs

Instructions

  1. Place chicken on baking sheet.
  2. Combine mayo and cheese and spread on chicken.
  3. Sprinkle on bread crumbs.
  4. 425 - 20 min.
