Peach Melba Icy Delight

Notes

  • Yield: 1 serving
  • Prep: 5 min
  • Total: 6 min

Ingredients

  • 1 scoop vanilla ice cream
  • 1 peach, sliced
  • 1 tbsp raspberry jam
  • 2 tbsp thawed Cool Whip Whipped Topping
  • 1 crushed Nilla Wafer

Instructions

  1. Place 1 scoop vanilla ice cream in a dessert bowl.
  2. Top with the sliced peach.
  3. Drizzle with raspberry jam, then top with thawed whipped topping.
  4. Sprinkle the crushed Nilla Wafer over the top and serve immediately.
