Perfect Peanut Butter Blossoms

Notes

  • Yield: 36 cookies
  • Prep: 10 min
  • Cook: 10 min
  • Total: 20 min

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup salted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar, plus 1/4 cup for rolling
  • 1/2 cup light brown sugar, firmly packed
  • 1/2 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tbsp milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 36 unwrapped Hershey’s Kisses (a little less than one bag)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper. Unwrap the Hershey’s Kisses and set aside.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the butter, 1/2 cup granulated sugar, brown sugar, peanut butter, egg, milk, vanilla extract, flour, baking soda, and salt. Mix with an electric mixer just until combined; do not overmix.
  3. Scoop out small, tablespoon-sized amounts of dough and roll into balls between your palms. Roll each ball in the extra 1/4 cup sugar in a shallow bowl, then place on the prepared baking sheet, spaced 1 to 2 inches apart.
  4. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes. Do not overbake; remove the cookies when they are just set (around 8–9 minutes).
  5. Immediately upon removing the cookies from the oven, gently press a Hershey’s Kiss into the center of each cookie. The chocolate will soften from the heat of the cookie and then firm up again as the cookies cool.
  6. Cool completely, then store in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 4 days.
