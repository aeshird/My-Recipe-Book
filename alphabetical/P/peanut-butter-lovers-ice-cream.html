Peanut Butter Lover’s Ice Cream

Notes

  • Yield: 2 servings (230 Calories each)

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup low-fat cottage cheese
  • 2 tbsp unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/4 cup powdered peanut butter
  • 1/2 serving (16 g) CSE Simply Vanilla Protein Powder
  • Vanilla stevia drops, optional
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 150 g frozen banana slices
  • 1/4 tsp xanthan gum (optional, to thicken)
  • 8–10 ice cubes (about 130 g)
  • 8 g mini chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Add the cottage cheese, 2 tbsp almond milk, powdered peanut butter, protein powder, and stevia drops (if using) to a blender. Blend until smooth; the mixture should be thick.
  2. Pour the mixture into an ice cube tray and freeze for at least 4 hours, or overnight.
  3. Add the 1/2 cup almond milk, the frozen cubes, frozen banana slices, xanthan gum (if using), and ice to a high-powered blender. Blend until smooth.
  4. Pour into two bowls and top each serving with 4 g mini chocolate chips. Serve immediately.
