Peanut Butter Cookie Shake

Notes

  • Yield: 1 serving (335 Calories)

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsweetened vanilla almond milk
  • 1 serving (33 g) CSE Simply Vanilla Protein Powder
  • 40 g frozen banana slices
  • 2 tbsp old-fashioned rolled oats
  • 1 tbsp natural peanut butter
  • Dash of sea salt
  • 6â€“8 ice cubes (about 120 g)

Topping

  • 1 tsp sugar in the raw

Instructions

  1. Add all of the shake ingredients to a high-powered blender.
  2. Blend on high until smooth.
  3. Pour into a cup and sprinkle with sugar in the raw before serving.
