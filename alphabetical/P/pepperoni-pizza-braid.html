Pepperoni Pizza Braid

Notes

  • Yield: 8 servings
  • Prep: 10 min
  • Total: 30 min

Ingredients

  • 1 can (8 oz) refrigerated Pillsbury Original Crescent Rolls (8 count)
  • 1/4 cup pizza sauce (from a 14 oz jar)
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese (4 oz)
  • 1/2 cup pepperoni slices (2 oz, about 27 slices)
  • Additional pizza sauce, for serving (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat oven to 375°F (190°C). Spray a large cookie sheet with cooking spray or line with parchment paper.
  2. Unroll the dough on the cookie sheet and press into a 13 x 8-inch rectangle. If using crescent rolls, firmly press perforations to seal. Spread pizza sauce in a 3 1/2-inch-wide strip lengthwise down the center of the dough, all the way to the ends.
  3. Layer half of the pepperoni slices and cheese over the sauce; repeat with the remaining pepperoni and cheese.
  4. Make cuts 1 inch apart on each side of the rectangle just to the edge of the filling. Alternating from side to side, fold cut strips of dough at an angle halfway across the filling, slightly overlapping the ends to form a braid.
  5. Bake for 16 to 20 minutes, or until deeply golden brown. Serve with additional pizza sauce, if desired.
