Mushroom Lasagna Roll Ups in Creamy Gorgonzola Cauliflower Sauce

Notes

  • Yield: 6 servings
  • Prep: 10 min | Cook: 1 hr | Total: 1 hr 10 min

Mushroom Mixture

  • 2 tbsp butter or oil
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 8 oz mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1 tsp thyme, chopped
  • 1/4 cup white wine or broth
  • 1 bunch kale, chopped

Cauliflower Sauce

  • 1 tbsp butter or olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 6 cups cauliflower florets (1 small head or 12 oz bag)
  • 2 cups vegetable broth or water
  • 1/2 cup gorgonzola or other blue cheese
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1/2 cup milk

Lasagna Roll Ups

  • 9 lasagna noodles, cooked (use gluten-free if needed)
  • 2 cups ricotta
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmigiano-Reggiano (Parmesan)
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella

Instructions

  1. Make the mushroom mixture: Melt the butter in a pan over medium heat. Add the onions and mushrooms and cook until the mushrooms start to caramelize, about 15–20 minutes.
  2. Add the garlic and thyme and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  3. Add the wine and deglaze the pan, scraping up any browned bits.
  4. Add the kale and cook until tender, about 10 minutes. Remove from heat and set aside.
  5. Make the cauliflower sauce: In a separate pot, heat butter or olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped garlic and sauté for 1–2 minutes until fragrant.
  6. Add cauliflower florets and broth (or water). Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer until the cauliflower is very tender, 10–15 minutes.
  7. Transfer the cauliflower and some of the cooking liquid to a blender. Add gorgonzola cheese, milk, salt, and pepper. Blend until smooth, adding more cooking liquid as needed to reach a thick, creamy sauce consistency.
  8. Adjust seasoning with additional salt and pepper if needed.
  9. Prepare the noodles & filling: Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions. Drain and lay them flat on a lightly oiled surface to prevent sticking.
  10. In a bowl, combine ricotta and Parmesan.
  11. Spread each lasagna noodle with a layer of the ricotta mixture. Spoon some of the mushroom-kale mixture along the length of each noodle and roll up tightly.
  12. Preheat oven to 375°F. Spread a thin layer of cauliflower sauce in the bottom of a baking dish.
  13. Arrange the lasagna roll ups seam-side down in the dish. Pour remaining cauliflower sauce over the top, then sprinkle with shredded mozzarella.
  14. Cover with foil and bake for 20 minutes. Remove foil and bake an additional 10–15 minutes, or until bubbly and golden.
  15. Let rest a few minutes before serving.
