Mini Meatloaves

Notes

  • Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 1 jar (12 oz) beef gravy, divided
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 lb ground beef
  • 1 package (6.25 oz) Stove Top Stuffing Mix for Beef or Chicken
  • 2 eggs, lightly beaten

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400Â°F. Line two baking sheets with foil.
  2. Pour 1/4 cup of the beef gravy into a large bowl. Stir in the water.
  3. Add ground beef, stuffing mix, and eggs to the bowl. Mix lightly until just combined.
  4. Divide the mixture in half. Shape one half into 2 oval loaves on one of the prepared baking sheets.
  5. Shape the remaining half into 16 meatballs and place them in a single layer on the second baking sheet.
  6. Bake both pans side by side for 20 minutes. Remove the meatballs from the oven and set aside.
  7. Bake the mini meatloaves an additional 10 minutes or until no longer pink in the centers.
  8. Slice the meatloaves and serve with your favorite vegetables. Reserve the meatballs and remaining gravy for Swedish Meatballs.
