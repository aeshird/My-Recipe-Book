Mocha Chocolate Chip Oats

Notes

  • Yield: 1 serving (350 Calories)

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup old-fashioned rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 3 tbsp (46 g) liquid egg whites
  • 1 tbsp (14 g) OffBeat Almond Mocha Butter
  • 3/4 serving (24 g) CSE Brownie Batter Protein Powder
  • 10 g dark chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Add oats, water, and liquid egg whites to a bowl. Microwave for 1 1/2 minutes.
  2. Stir in the nut butter until melted and combined. Then add the protein powder and stir until well combined.
  3. Top with dark chocolate chips and serve warm.
