Mushroom Stroganoff (Vegetarian)

Notes

  • Yield: 4 servings
  • Prep: 15 min | Cook: 20 min | Total: 35 min

Ingredients

  • 10 oz wide egg noodles
  • 4 tbsp olive oil, divided
  • 20 oz mixed mushrooms (such as portobello and white), thinly sliced
  • 1/2 large white onion, diced
  • 5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 tsp salt, divided
  • 1/2 tsp ground pepper
  • 2 cups vegetable broth, divided
  • 3 tbsp white whole wheat flour
  • 1/2 cup 2% Greek yogurt (or sour cream)

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook egg noodles until al dente according to package instructions. Drain, rinse with cold water, and set aside.
  2. Heat 3 tbsp olive oil in a large skillet or Dutch oven over medium-high heat.
  3. When the oil is hot, add all mushrooms and 1/4 tsp salt. Reduce heat to medium-low and sauté 6–8 minutes, tossing every few minutes, until mushrooms are browned and have released their liquid.
  4. Add the remaining 1 tbsp olive oil, diced onion, and garlic. Sauté for 2–3 minutes, until onions are softened.
  5. In a small bowl, whisk the flour into 1 cup of vegetable broth until mostly dissolved.
  6. Turn heat up to medium-high. Slowly pour the flour-broth mixture into the pan, whisking constantly until the mixture thickens.
  7. Add the remaining 1 cup vegetable broth, remaining 1/4 tsp salt, and pepper. Simmer for a few minutes until the sauce is smooth and slightly thickened.
  8. Remove pan from heat and let cool for 1–2 minutes so the Greek yogurt won’t curdle.
  9. Stir in Greek yogurt until fully incorporated and creamy.
  10. Add the cooked egg noodles to the pan and toss to coat in the sauce.
  11. Taste and adjust seasoning if needed. Serve warm.
