Mustard Fried Bacon Burger

Notes

  • Yield: 6 servings

Burgers & Toppings

  • 16 oz lean (93/7) ground beef
  • 2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tsp yellow mustard
  • 2 tsp dried minced onions
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp sea salt
  • Dash black pepper
  • 4 slices turkey bacon, chopped
  • 1 sliced sweet onion
  • 4 whole wheat buns
  • Butter lettuce
  • 8 tomato slices
  • 12 dill pickle slices

Sauce Toppings

  • 1/2 tbsp olive oil mayo (per burger)
  • 1/2 tbsp ketchup (per burger)

Instructions

  1. Heat grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a bowl, combine ground beef, Worcestershire sauce, mustard, dried minced onion, garlic powder, sea salt, and black pepper. Mix with your hands until well combined.
  3. Weigh the mixture and divide by four to form four equal-sized burger patties.
  4. Place the burger patties and turkey bacon slices on the grill. Cook burgers for 5â€“8 minutes per side, or until desired doneness. Cook bacon until crispy, then chop if not already chopped.
  5. Heat a frying pan over medium-high heat, lightly grease, and add the sliced onion. Cook, stirring and flipping, until soft and golden on all sides.
  6. To assemble each burger, layer the bottom bun with lettuce, a burger patty, tomato slices, pickle slices, grilled onions, and crispy bacon.
  7. Spread mayo and ketchup on the top bun, then place on top of the burger. Serve immediately.
