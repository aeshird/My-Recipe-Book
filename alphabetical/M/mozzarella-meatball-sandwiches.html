Mozzarella Meatball Sandwiches

Notes

  • Yield: 4 servings
  • Prep: 5 min | Cook: 10–20 min | Total: ~20 min

Ingredients

  • 1 loaf (11.75 oz) Pepperidge Farm Frozen Mozzarella & Garlic Cheese Bread
  • 1/2 cup Prego Traditional Pasta Sauce
  • 12 (1/2 oz each) or 6 (1 oz each) frozen meatballs

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. Remove the frozen cheese bread from the bag. Place halves, cut-side up, on an ungreased baking sheet. If frozen together, gently separate with a fork.
  3. Bake on the middle oven rack for about 10 minutes, or until heated through.
  4. Meanwhile, place pasta sauce and meatballs in a medium saucepan. Cook over medium heat for about 20 minutes, or until meatballs are heated through.
  5. Place the hot meatballs along the bottom bread half and spoon over some of the sauce.
  6. Top with the remaining bread half. Cut into quarters and serve.
