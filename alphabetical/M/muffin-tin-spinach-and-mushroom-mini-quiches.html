Muffin-Tin Spinach & Mushroom Mini Quiches

Notes

  • Yield: 6 servings
  • Prep: 35 min | Total: 1 hr 5 min

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 8 oz mixed wild mushrooms (such as oyster and shiitake), sliced
  • 1 cup thinly sliced yellow onion
  • 1 tbsp minced garlic
  • 2 tsp minced fresh thyme
  • 1 (5 oz) package fresh spinach, coarsely chopped
  • 8 large eggs
  • 2/3 cup whole milk
  • 2 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp ground pepper
  • 3/4 cup shredded Gruyère cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat. Add mushrooms in an even layer and cook, undisturbed, for about 4 minutes until browned on the bottom.
  3. Stir and continue cooking, stirring occasionally, until browned all over, about 5 more minutes.
  4. Add the onion and cook, stirring occasionally, until beginning to soften, about 4 minutes.
  5. Stir in garlic and thyme and cook, stirring, until fragrant, about 2 minutes.
  6. Add chopped spinach and cook, stirring constantly, until just wilted, about 2 minutes. Remove from heat.
  7. In a large bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, Dijon mustard, salt, and pepper. Stir in Gruyère and the mushroom-spinach mixture.
  8. Coat a standard 12-cup muffin tin with cooking spray. Divide the egg mixture evenly among the cups.
  9. Bake, uncovered, for about 30 minutes, or until puffed and set in the center.
  10. Remove from the pan and serve immediately.
