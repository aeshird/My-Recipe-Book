Mascarpone & Berries Toast

Notes

  • Yield: 1 serving
  • Prep: 5 min | Total: 5 min

Ingredients

  • 1 slice whole-grain bread, toasted
  • 2 tbsp mascarpone cheese
  • 1/4 cup berries (raspberries, blueberries, and/or chopped strawberries)
  • 1 tsp mint leaves, chopped or torn

Instructions

  1. Toast the whole-grain bread to your liking.
  2. Spread mascarpone cheese evenly over the toast.
  3. Top with berries and garnish with mint leaves.
  4. Serve immediately.
