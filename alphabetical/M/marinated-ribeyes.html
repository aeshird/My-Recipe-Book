Marinated Ribeyes

Notes

  • Yield: 4 servings
  • Prep: 10 min + marinating | Grill: 10 min

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup barbecue sauce
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tbsp steak sauce
  • 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tsp steak seasoning
  • 1 tsp hot pepper sauce
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 4 beef ribeye steaks (8 oz each)

Instructions

  1. In a large resealable plastic bag, combine the barbecue sauce, olive oil, Worcestershire sauce, steak sauce, red wine vinegar, soy sauce, steak seasoning, hot pepper sauce, and minced garlic.
  2. Add the ribeye steaks to the bag, seal, and turn to coat. Refrigerate for 4 hours or overnight.
  3. Preheat grill to medium-hot heat.
  4. Remove the steaks from the marinade and discard the remaining marinade.
  5. Grill steaks, covered, for 5–7 minutes on each side or until desired doneness is reached (145°F for medium-rare, 160°F for medium, 170°F for well-done).
  6. Let rest a few minutes before serving.
