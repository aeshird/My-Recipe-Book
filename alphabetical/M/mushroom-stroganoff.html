Mushroom Stroganoff

Notes

  • Yield: 6 servings
  • Cook: 15 min

Ingredients

  • 16 oz sliced portobello mushrooms
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 diced white or yellow onion
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 16 oz long fusilli pasta, cooked al dente (or pasta of choice)
  • Parmesan shavings, for garnish
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta according to package directions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pot over medium-high heat, add olive oil, garlic, and onion. Sauté 2–3 minutes or until onions are translucent.
  3. Add sliced mushrooms and sauté an additional 2–3 minutes.
  4. Season with salt and pepper, then add the vegetable broth. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low.
  5. Stir in the heavy cream and sour cream. Add the cooked pasta and grated Parmesan cheese and stir until everything is well combined and the sauce is creamy.
  6. Let sit a few minutes to thicken slightly, then taste and adjust seasoning as needed.
  7. Serve hot, garnished with Parmesan shavings.
