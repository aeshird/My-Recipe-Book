Healthy Fruit & Oats Smoothie

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quartered strawberries
  • 1 sliced banana
  • 1/4 cup raw almonds
  • 1/2 cup oats
  • 1 cup low-fat vanilla yogurt
  • 1 tsp cinnamon honey

Instructions

  1. Add strawberries, banana, almonds, oats, vanilla yogurt, and cinnamon honey to a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Adjust thickness with a splash of milk or water if desired. Serve immediately.
