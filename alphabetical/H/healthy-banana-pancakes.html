Healthy Banana Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 bananas
  • 1 cup oats
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Add eggs, bananas, oats, vanilla extract, cinnamon, and salt to a blender.
  2. Blend until the mixture is smooth.
  3. Heat a nonstick pan or griddle over medium heat and lightly grease if needed.
  4. Pour small amounts of batter onto the pan to form pancakes.
  5. Cook until bubbles form on the surface and the edges look set, then flip and cook the other side until golden brown.
  6. Serve warm and garnish with fruit, maple syrup, Nutella, or your favorite toppings.
