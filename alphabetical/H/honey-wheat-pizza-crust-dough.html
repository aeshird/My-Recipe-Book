Honey Wheat Pizza Crust Dough

Notes

  • Yield: 6 servings (500 Calories each at 8 oz dough per pizza)

Ingredients

  • 715 g all-purpose unbleached white wheat flour
  • 1 1/2 tbsp active dry yeast
  • 1 1/2 tsp sea salt
  • 2 cups warm water (100–110°F)
  • 1 1/2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1/4 cup raw honey

Instructions

  1. Add flour, yeast, and sea salt to a large mixing bowl. Stir until combined.
  2. Add warm water, olive oil, and honey. Mix until everything is well combined; the dough will be sticky.
  3. Knead the dough for about 5 minutes using a bread mixer, a stand mixer with dough hook, or your hands.
  4. Transfer dough to a greased bowl, turning once to coat. Cover with a towel and let rise for 30–60 minutes, or until doubled in size.
  5. Weigh out 8 oz of dough for each pizza; this recipe should make 6 personal-sized pizzas.
  6. Place a portion of dough on a floured surface and roll out into a circle about 8 inches across. Fold in the edges to create a crust.
  7. Place the dough directly onto the grill or on a baking sheet. Top with your favorite pizza toppings.
  8. Bake or grill for about 10–15 minutes, or until the crust is golden around the edges and toppings are cooked to your liking.
