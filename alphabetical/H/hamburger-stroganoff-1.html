Hamburger Stroganoff 1

Notes

  • Yield: 4 servings (about 1 cup each)
  • Prep: 10 min
  • Cook: 25 min

Ingredients

  • 1 lb lean ground beef
  • 1 medium onion, chopped (about 1/2 cup)
  • 1 clove garlic, finely chopped
  • 1 can (10 3/4 oz) condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup sour cream or plain yogurt
  • Hot cooked noodles or rice, for serving (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a 10-inch skillet over medium heat, cook the beef, onion, and garlic for 8â€“10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the beef is browned. Drain any excess fat.
  2. Stir in the cream of mushroom soup and salt.
  3. Simmer uncovered for about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Stir in sour cream and cook just until hot (do not boil).
  5. Serve over hot cooked noodles or rice if desired.
