Ham & Cheese Casserole

Ingredients

  • 1 (8 oz) package medium noodles, cooked and drained
  • 2 cups cubed ham
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 can cream of celery soup
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup chopped green pepper (optional)
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion (or 1 tsp onion powder)
  • Pepper, to taste
  • Breadcrumbs, for topping

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Grease a 9 x 13-inch pan.
  2. Layer half of the noodles, ham, and cheese in the pan.
  3. In a bowl, combine the cream of celery soup, sour cream, green pepper (if using), onion or onion powder, and pepper. Spread half of this mixture over the first layer.
  4. Repeat layers with remaining noodles, ham, cheese, and soup mixture.
  5. Sprinkle breadcrumbs evenly over the top.
  6. Bake, uncovered, for 40–45 minutes, or until hot and bubbly. Depending on your oven, 30 minutes may be sufficient.
  7. Let stand a few minutes before serving. Note: You can use pre-cubed ham from the dairy section for convenience.
