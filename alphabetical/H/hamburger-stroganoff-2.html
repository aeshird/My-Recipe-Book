Hamburger Stroganoff 2

Ingredients

  • 1 lb hamburger
  • 1 medium onion, chopped (about 1/2 cup)
  • 1/4 cup margarine or butter
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 clove garlic, finely chopped, or 1 tsp garlic salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • 1 can (4 oz) mushroom stems and pieces, drained
  • 1 can (10 3/4 oz) condensed cream of chicken soup
  • 1 cup dairy sour cream or unflavored yogurt
  • 2â€“3 cups hot cooked noodles or rice

Instructions

  1. In a 10-inch skillet, cook and stir the hamburger and onion in margarine over medium heat until the hamburger is light brown.
  2. Stir in flour, salt, garlic or garlic salt, pepper, and mushrooms. Cook, stirring constantly, for 5 minutes.
  3. Stir in the cream of chicken soup. Heat to boiling, stirring constantly, then reduce heat.
  4. Simmer uncovered for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Stir in sour cream and heat through (do not boil).
  6. Serve over hot noodles or rice. If desired, sprinkle with snipped parsley.
