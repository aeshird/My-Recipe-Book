Snickers Power Bites

Notes

  • Yield: 28 servings
  • With cocoa dusting: 100 calories per serving
  • With chocolate coating: 110 calories per serving

Ingredients

  • 1 cup natural crunchy peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup raw honey
  • 1 serving CSE Caramel Toffee Protein Powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Dash of sea salt
  • 1 1/2 cups old-fashioned rolled oats

Cocoa Dusting

  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder

Chocolate Coating

  • 120 g dark chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Add the peanut butter, honey, protein powder, vanilla, sea salt, and oats to a large bowl. Mix until well combined.
  2. Using a small cookie scoop or your hands, scoop and roll the mixture into balls.
  3. If using the cocoa dusting method: Add the cocoa powder to a shallow bowl. Roll each ball in the cocoa until evenly coated. Place in a container and store in the fridge or freezer.
  4. If using the chocolate coating method: Place the dark chocolate chips in a microwave-safe bowl. Microwave in 30-second increments, stirring after each, until melted and smooth.
  5. Roll each ball in the melted chocolate, then lift out with a fork or slotted spoon, tapping gently on the bowl to remove excess chocolate. Place the coated bites on a parchment-lined baking sheet and refrigerate or freeze until set.
