Spinach Ravioli with Artichokes & Olives

Notes

  • Prep: 15 min | Total: 15 min
  • Yield: 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 2 (8 oz) packages frozen or refrigerated spinach-and-ricotta ravioli
  • 1/2 cup oil-packed sun-dried tomatoes, drained (2 tablespoons oil reserved)
  • 1 (10 oz) package frozen quartered artichoke hearts, thawed
  • 1 (15 oz) can no-salt-added cannellini beans, rinsed
  • 1/4 cup Kalamata olives, sliced
  • 3 tablespoons toasted pine nuts
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Cook the ravioli according to package directions. Drain and toss with 1 tablespoon of the reserved sun-dried tomato oil. Set aside.
  2. Heat the remaining 1 tablespoon of reserved oil in a large nonstick skillet over medium heat. Add the artichokes and cannellini beans and sauté until heated through, 2–3 minutes.
  3. Fold in the cooked ravioli, sun-dried tomatoes, olives, pine nuts, and basil. Toss gently to combine.
  4. Serve warm.
