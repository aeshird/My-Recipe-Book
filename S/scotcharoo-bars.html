Scotcharoo Bars

Notes

  • Yield: 16 servings

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup OffBeat Sweet Classic Peanut Butter
  • 1/3 cup powdered peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup raw honey
  • 1 tbsp flaxseed meal
  • 1 tbsp unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • Dash sea salt
  • 1/2 serving CSE Simply Vanilla Protein Powder
  • 1/4 cup butterscotch flavored chips
  • 2/3 cup crispy rice cereal

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine peanut butter, powdered peanut butter, honey, flaxseed meal, almond milk, sea salt, and protein powder. Stir until well mixed and smooth.
  2. Melt the butterscotch chips in a microwave-safe bowl or in a saucepan over low heat, stirring until smooth.
  3. Add the melted butterscotch and crispy rice cereal to the peanut butter mixture. Stir until everything is evenly combined.
  4. Press the mixture firmly into the bottom of an 8x8-inch baking pan.
  5. Refrigerate until set. Cut into 16 squares.
  6. Serve as-is, or place on a fondue tray and dip in chocolate if desired.
