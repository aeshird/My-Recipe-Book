Skillet Creamy Garlic Chicken with Broccoli

Notes

  • Prep: 10 min | Cook: 20 min | Total: 30 min
  • Yield: 6 servings

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds boneless chicken breasts
  • Kosher salt, to taste
  • Fresh cracked black pepper, to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 4 tablespoons butter, divided
  • 4 to 5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 10.5 ounces (1 can) condensed cream of chicken soup (or more, if needed)
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 to 3 cups chopped broccoli
  • 1 teaspoon chopped fresh parsley
  • Lemon wedges, optional

Instructions

  1. Season the chicken with salt, pepper, and paprika. Set aside.
  2. Heat a large pan over medium-high heat. Melt 2 tablespoons of the butter. Add the chicken and cook for about 5 minutes on each side, or until browned. Remove the chicken from the pan, cover, and keep warm.
  3. In the same pan, melt the remaining 2 tablespoons of butter. Add the garlic and cook until lightly browned and fragrant.
  4. Whisk in the condensed cream of chicken soup and the water. Cook until the mixture is hot and bubbly, then reduce the heat to low.
  5. Add the chicken and broccoli back into the pan. Cover with a lid and cook for about 10 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the broccoli is tender. (For firmer, more al dente broccoli, add the broccoli during the last few minutes of cooking.)
  6. Serve with rice or pasta and lemon wedges. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley.
