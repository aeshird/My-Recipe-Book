California Chicken

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken breasts
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp water
  • 2 tsp lemon pepper seasoning
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp basil
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder

Instructions

  1. Combine ingredients and brush both sides of chicken with mixture.
  2. Saute chicken in non-stick skillet till lightly browned and thoroughly cooked.
