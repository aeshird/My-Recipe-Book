Tortilla Wraps

Ingredients

  • Large flour tortillas
  • Cooked bacon
  • Deli ham slices
  • Deli turkey slices
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato, sliced
  • Cheddar cheese, sliced

Instructions

  1. Place a tortilla on a cutting board. Make a single cut from the center of the tortilla to the edge closest to you.
  2. Visualize the tortilla divided into four quarters. Starting in the bottom right quarter and working counterclockwise, add the fillings:
    • Bottom right: cooked bacon
    • Top right: deli ham and turkey
    • Top left: lettuce and tomato
    • Bottom left: sliced cheddar cheese
  3. To fold, grab the bottom right corner (bacon section) and fold it up over the ham and turkey quarter.
  4. Flip that section over the top left quarter (lettuce and tomato), then fold it down over the bottom left quarter (cheddar cheese).
  5. You should end up with a triangle-shaped wrap with all the fillings layered. Grill in a panini press or in a skillet (optional) or serve as is.
