Taco Salad

Notes

  • Yield: 4 servings
  • Approx. 350 Calories per serving

Ingredients

  • 10 oz lean ground beef
  • 1 cup yellow onion, chopped
  • 2 tbsp taco seasoning
  • 8 cups green leaf lettuce (about 2 cups per serving)
  • 1 cup black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 cup corn
  • 1 cup tomatoes, chopped
  • 4 tbsp low-fat Mexican shredded cheese
  • 1/2 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup salsa
  • Green onions, sliced (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Brown the ground beef and onions together in a frying pan over medium-high heat. Stir in the taco seasoning, mix well, and remove from heat. Weigh the meat mixture and divide the total weight by 4 to determine the amount needed for each serving.
  2. For each salad, pile 2 cups of lettuce in a bowl. Top with 1/4 of the meat mixture, 1/4 cup black beans, 1/4 cup corn, 1/4 cup tomatoes, and 1 tbsp cheese.
  3. Top each salad with 2 tbsp plain Greek yogurt and 2 tbsp salsa to use as the dressing. Garnish with sliced green onions and serve immediately.
