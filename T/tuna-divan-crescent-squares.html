Tuna Divan Crescent Squares

Notes

  • Yield: 8 servings
  • Prep Time: 20 minutes
  • Total Time: 55 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 can (8 oz) refrigerated crescent dinner rolls
  • 1 cup shredded Swiss cheese (4 oz)
  • 1 box (9 oz) frozen broccoli cuts, cooked and well drained
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 can (10 3/4 oz) condensed cream of broccoli soup
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise or salad dressing
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp dried dill weed
  • 1 can (5 or 6 oz) tuna in water, drained
  • 1 jar (12 oz) diced pimientos, drained

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Unroll the crescent dough on a work surface and separate it into 2 long rectangles. Place the rectangles lengthwise in an ungreased 13x9-inch baking pan. Press the dough in the bottom and 1/2 inch up the sides to form a crust, pressing edges and perforations to seal.
  3. Sprinkle the shredded Swiss cheese evenly over the crust, then arrange the cooked, drained broccoli over the cheese.
  4. In a medium bowl, lightly beat the eggs with a whisk. Whisk in the condensed cream of broccoli soup, mayonnaise, onion powder, and dried dill weed until smooth.
  5. Stir in the tuna and diced pimientos. Pour the mixture evenly over the broccoli in the pan.
  6. Bake for 28â€“32 minutes, or until the filling is set and the crust is golden.
  7. Allow to cool slightly, then cut into squares and serve warm.

Notes / Variations

  • Substitute 1 can (5 or 6 oz) chicken, drained, for the tuna if desired.
  • You can also use cream of chicken soup instead of cream of broccoli soup when using chicken.
