Tropical Orange Sunrise Smoothie

Notes

  • Yield: 2 servings
  • Approx. 195 Calories per serving

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsweetened vanilla almond milk
  • 50 g frozen bananas
  • 120 g frozen pineapple
  • 120 g clementine oranges (peeled segments)
  • 1/4 cup low-fat cottage cheese
  • 2 scoops Vanilla CSE Kids Shake
  • Liquid vanilla stevia drops, to taste
  • 120 g ice

Instructions

  1. Add the almond milk, frozen bananas, frozen pineapple, clementine segments, cottage cheese, Vanilla CSE Kids Shake, stevia drops, and ice to a blender.
  2. Blend until completely smooth and creamy.
  3. Taste and adjust sweetness with additional stevia if desired. Pour into 2 glasses and serve immediately.
