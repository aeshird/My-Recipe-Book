Tomato Basil Pasta with Grilled Chicken

Ingredients

  • 2 cups grilled chicken, cubed
  • 2 cups grape tomatoes, halved
  • 3 cups uncooked penne pasta
  • 3 1/2 cups low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
  • 1/2 cup fresh basil, chopped, divided
  • 1 tsp minced garlic
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • Cooking spray

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 450°F (230°C). Lightly spray a 9x13-inch baking dish with cooking spray.
  2. In the prepared baking dish, combine the grilled chicken, grape tomatoes, uncooked penne, chicken broth, 1 cup of the mozzarella cheese, 1/4 cup chopped basil, minced garlic, and salt. Stir until everything is evenly distributed.
  3. Cover the dish tightly with foil and bake for 40 minutes.
  4. Remove the foil and sprinkle the remaining 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese over the top. Bake uncovered for an additional 5â€“10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly and the pasta is tender.
  5. Garnish with the remaining 1/4 cup fresh basil before serving.
