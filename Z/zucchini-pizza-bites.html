Zucchini Pizza Bites

Notes

  • Yield: 12–16 pizza bites
  • Prep Time: 10 minutes
  • Cook Time: 15 minutes
  • Total Time: 25 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 large or 2 small zucchini (about 8 oz)
  • 1/2 cup marinara sauce
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 6 basil leaves
  • Shredded Parmesan cheese for topping (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Using a mandoline or sharp knife, slice the zucchini into 1/2-inch-thick rounds.
  3. Lay the zucchini rounds on a paper towel and sprinkle lightly with salt. Let sit for a few minutes, then dab the tops with another paper towel to remove excess moisture.
  4. Arrange the zucchini slices in a single layer on the prepared baking sheet.
  5. Spoon a small amount of marinara sauce onto each zucchini slice.
  6. Top each slice with shredded mozzarella cheese.
  7. Bake for 10–15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly and the zucchini is tender.
  8. Remove from the oven and top with torn basil leaves and a sprinkle of shredded Parmesan, if desired. Serve warm.
