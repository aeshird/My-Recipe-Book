Zesty Chicken Salad

Salad Ingredients

  • 10 oz raw chicken breast
  • 8 cups spring salad mix
  • 14 oz chopped tomatoes
  • 14 oz sliced red bell peppers
  • 1 cup unsweetened dried cranberries
  • 1/2 cup sliced green onions
  • 4 tbsp slivered almonds
  • 4 tbsp feta cheese crumbles

Zesty Lemon Cilantro Dressing

  • 2 tbsp light-tasting olive oil
  • 2 tbsp raw honey
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp white wine vinegar
  • 2 tsp lemon zest
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1/4 tsp sea salt
  • 1/4 cup cilantro

Instructions

  1. Place all dressing ingredients into a blender and blend until smooth. Refrigerate until ready to serve.
  2. Heat the oven to 400°F (205°C). Place the sliced red bell peppers on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Spray the tops with cooking spray, sprinkle with sea salt, and roast for 15 minutes. Remove and let cool slightly.
  3. Grill the chicken breast until both sides are browned and the juices run clear, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C). Let rest for a few minutes, then slice.
  4. In a large bowl or on individual plates, arrange the spring salad mix.
  5. Top with chopped tomatoes, roasted red bell peppers, dried cranberries, sliced green onions, slivered almonds, and feta cheese crumbles.
  6. Add the sliced grilled chicken over the top of the salad.
  7. Drizzle with the chilled lemon cilantro dressing just before serving.
