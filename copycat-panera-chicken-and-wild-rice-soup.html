Copycat Panera Chicken & Wild Rice Soup

Ingredients

  1. 1 small carrot, finely diced
  2. 1 stalk celery, finely diced
  3. 1 TB olive oil
  4. 1 1/2 Tbsp minced garlic
  5. 2 cups chicken stock
  6. 1 cup heavy cream, divided
  7. 2 cups water
  8. 2 cups whole milk, divided
  9. 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  10. 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  11. 1 dried bay leaf
  12. 2 boneless skinless chicken breasts, cooked and shredded
  13. 1/2 cup all purpose floud
  14. 1 4.3oz box of Rice a Roni Long Grain and Wild Rice + seasoning packet
  15. Salt and pepper, to taste
  16. Scallions

Instructions

  1. In a large stock pot over medium heat, heat olive oil and saute carrots, onion, celery until onions are tender, or about, 10 minutes.
  2. Stir in garlic, chicken stock, water and 1 cup of the milk and 1/2 cup of heavy cream until well combined.
  3. Add pepper, oregano, bay leaf, and chicken.
  4. Simmer soup over medium heat for 15 minutes.
  5. In a small bowl, whisk together remaining 1 cup of milk, 1/2 cup of cream and flour until smooth.
  6. Pour flour mixture into the soup and stir until well blended.
  7. Add the box of rice, along with the packet, to the soup and stir.
  8. Simmer soup for an additional 20 minutes, or until the rice is cooked.
  9. Add salt and pepper and top with scallions.
