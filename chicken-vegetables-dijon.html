Chicken Vegetables Dijon

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken
  • 1 cr. chicken soup
  • 3 tbsp dijon mustard
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 pkg. (16 oz) frozen unseasoned or garlic seasoned pasta and vegetable combination
  • 2 cans (2.8 oz) french fried onions
  • 1 medium tomato, cut into wedges

Instructions

  1. Place chicken in 12 x 8 baking dish.
  2. In medium bowl, stir soup, mustard, pepper, and milk until smooth
  3. Spoon 1 cup soup mixture over chicken.
  4. 375 - 30 min.
  5. In large bowl, combine pasta and vegetable, 1 can onions and remaining soup mixture.
  6. Spoon pasta mixture under and around chicken. Bake 20 min. or until chicken is fork-tender and sauce is bubbling.
  7. Top with tomato wedges and 1 can onions; bake 3 min.
