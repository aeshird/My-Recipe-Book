Blueberry Muffins

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups flour
  • 1/3 cup sugar
  • 2 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup fresh or unthawed frozen berries
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1/3 cup melted margarine (or applesauce)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F. Grease muffin cups or line with paper liners.
  2. In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. Stir in berries.
  3. Add milk, egg, and melted margarine (or applesauce). Mix just until moistened. Batter will be lumpy; do not overbeat.
  4. Spoon batter into prepared muffin cups.
  5. Bake at 400°F for approximately 18 minutes, or until tops spring back when lightly touched.
  6. Serve warm.

Notes

  • Aunt Karen uses applesauce instead of melted margarine.
  • If making 12 muffins, you may need slightly less baking time; 18 minutes works well for 6 larger muffins.
