Beef, Bean & Tater Casserole

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • 2 cups French-style green beans, drained
    (#303 can or 16 oz frozen, thawed and drained)
  • 2 cans cheddar cheese soup (undiluted)
  • 1 lb tater tots (1 package)

Instructions

  1. Brown the ground beef and drain. Season with salt and pepper.
  2. Add the chopped onion and pat the beef mixture into the bottom of an 8x12-inch pan.
  3. Spread the well-drained green beans evenly over the meat.
  4. Spread the undiluted cheddar cheese soup over the beans.
  5. Top with tater tots.
  6. Bake at 350Â°F for 60 minutes.

Notes

  • You can use a 9x13-inch pan; the casserole will be a bit thinner and the potatoes may not completely cover the pan.
