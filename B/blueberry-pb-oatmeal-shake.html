Blueberry PB Oatmeal Shake

Notes

  • Yield: 1 serving (365 calories)

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup old-fashioned rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 46 g liquid egg whites
  • 1/2 cup frozen blueberries
  • 14 g OffBeat Sweet Classic Peanut Butter
  • 3/4 serving CSE Simply Vanilla Protein Powder
  • 20 g banana slices
  • 1 tbsp peanut butter powder

Instructions

  1. Add the oats, water, and egg whites to a bowl.
  2. Microwave for 1Â½ minutes, or cook on the stovetop until thickened.
  3. Stir in the blueberries and 10 g of the peanut butter.
  4. Add the protein powder and stir until well combined.
  5. Drizzle the remaining 4 g peanut butter over the oatmeal and top with banana slices and peanut butter powder.
