Buffalo Chicken Salad

Notes

  • Yield: 4 servings (340 calories per serving)

Ingredients

  • 8 oz cooked chicken breast (about 10.6 oz raw)
  • 2 tbsp Frank’s hot sauce
  • 4 cups romaine lettuce
  • 4 cups broccoli or cabbage slaw
  • 2 celery sticks, diced
  • 1 cup matchstick carrots
  • 20 cherry tomatoes
  • 160 g diced avocado
  • 4 tbsp diced red onion
  • 4 tbsp bleu cheese or feta cheese crumbles
  • 8 tbsp Bolthouse Farms Classic Ranch or Bleu Cheese dressing
  • 300 g apples

Instructions

  1. Cook the chicken (or use white meat from a rotisserie chicken) and dice or shred.
  2. Combine the chicken and Frank’s hot sauce in a bowl. Set aside.
  3. Chop the romaine lettuce, celery, cherry tomatoes, and red onion.
  4. In a large bowl, mix the lettuce, slaw, celery, carrots, tomatoes, onion, and buffalo chicken together (leave avocado and dressing off until serving).
  5. When ready to eat, portion salad onto plates. Top each serving with 40 g avocado and 2 tbsp dressing.
  6. Serve each salad with about 75 g apple slices on the side.
