Black Bean Tostadas

Notes

  • Yield: 4 servings (350 calories)
  • Enjoy 2 tostadas per serving.

Ingredients

  • 8 corn tortillas
  • 2/3 cup black beans or refried beans
  • 4 tsp taco seasoning
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 8 oz grilled chicken breast (about 10.6 oz raw)
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 8 tbsp low-fat shredded mozzarella cheese

Toppings

  • Shredded lettuce
  • 8 oz guacamole
  • 4 tbsp nonfat plain Greek yogurt
  • 4 tbsp salsa
  • Juice of 1 lime

Instructions

  1. Grill or cook the chicken according to your preferred method (or your Food Prep Guide). Slice or dice when done.
  2. Preheat the oven to 375°F.
  3. Line a baking sheet with foil and spray with cooking spray. Lay the tortillas in a single layer and spray the tops with cooking spray. Sprinkle the tops with taco seasoning. Bake for 8 minutes.
  4. Drain and rinse the beans, then microwave for 30–45 seconds and mash with a fork. Spread a rounded tablespoon of beans onto each warm tortilla.
  5. Slice the bell pepper, grilled chicken, and green onions. Layer the bell peppers evenly on the tortillas and top each one with about 1 oz grilled chicken, 1 tbsp shredded cheese, and some green onion.
  6. Return to the oven for another 8 minutes.
  7. Serve warm: 2 tostadas per serving with shredded lettuce, 2 oz guacamole, 1 tbsp plain Greek yogurt, 1 tbsp salsa, and a squeeze of lime juice.
